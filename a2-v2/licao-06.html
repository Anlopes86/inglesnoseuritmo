"What movie?"

A:

"No way! What were you doing when the lights came back on?"

"I was sitting in the dark, waiting!"

Grammar Focus: Past Continuous

Formation: was / were + verb-ing

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
I / He / She / Itwas watchingwasn't watchingWas I watching?
You / We / Theywere watchingweren't watchingWere they watching?

when — Interrupção

Use when para indicar que uma ação mais curta (Simple Past) interrompeu uma ação em andamento (Past Continuous).

I was cooking when the phone rang.

Eu estava cozinhando quando o telefone tocou.

She was sleeping when I arrived.

Ela estava dormindo quando eu cheguei.

while — Simultaneidade

Use while para indicar que duas ações aconteciam ao mesmo tempo no passado.

While I was studying, she was watching TV.

Enquanto eu estudava, ela assistia TV.

He was cooking while she was chatting.

Ele estava cozinhando enquanto ela conversava.

⚠️ Verbos de estado não usam -ing

Verbos como know, love, believe, want, need, understand normalmente não aparecem no Past Continuous: I was knowing ❌ → I knew ✅

Expressions & Phrasal Verbs

Expressões naturais para narrar situações e contar histórias.

Phrasal Verb

Break down

Significado: quebrar, parar de funcionar (máquinas/carros)

"I was driving home when my car broke down."

Eu estava dirigindo para casa quando meu carro quebrou.

Phrasal Verb

Run into

Significado: encontrar alguém por acaso

"While I was walking in the park, I ran into an old friend."

Enquanto eu caminhava no parque, encontrei um velho amigo por acaso.

Expression

Out of nowhere

Significado: do nada, de repente, sem aviso

"We were chatting when, out of nowhere, the alarm went off."

Estávamos conversando quando, do nada, o alarme disparou.

Expression

At that moment

Significado: naquele momento, naquela hora

"At that moment, everyone was laughing and having fun."

Naquele momento, todos estavam rindo e se divertindo.

Mini Dialogues

Situation 1 — A car problem

A:

"Why were you late?"

B:

"I was driving to work when my car broke down."

A:

"That's terrible! What were you doing while you waited?"

B:

"I was just sitting there, out of nowhere, in the rain!"

Situation 2 — An unexpected meeting

A:

"Guess what? I ran into Maria yesterday!"

B:

"Really? What were you doing?"

A:

"I was buying coffee. At that moment, she walked in!"

Situation 3 — A surprise party

A:

"Were you surprised at the party?"

B:

"Completely! I was watching TV and, out of nowhere, everyone was there!"

Situation 4 — At the office

A:

"What was everyone doing when the boss arrived?"

B:

"Some were working, some were chatting. At that moment, it got very quiet!"

Reading: The Blackout

Leitura guiada com past continuous, when, while e expressões da aula.

Text

Last Tuesday evening, the whole neighbourhood experienced a power outage. At 8 PM, people were doing all kinds of things. Some families were having dinner, children were doing their homework, and teenagers were chatting online.

Lucas was watching his favourite series when, out of nowhere, everything went dark. His sister Ana was cooking in the kitchen while their mother was reading a book. At that moment, the whole family stopped and looked at each other in the dark.

Lucas grabbed his phone to call the electricity company, but while he was looking for the number, he ran into his neighbour's message in the group chat. The neighbour's car had also broken down outside, blocking the street. It was quite a night!

Comprehension Questions

1. What was Lucas doing when the power went out?

2. What were Ana and their mother doing at that moment?

3. What was Lucas doing when he found the neighbour's message?

4. What had happened to the neighbour's car?
