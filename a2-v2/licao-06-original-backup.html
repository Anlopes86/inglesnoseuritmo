Past actions in progress

Describe what was happening at a specific time in the past.

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar o past continuous para contar o que estava acontecendo quando algo interrompeu a situação.

By the end, you can:

  • falar de ações em andamento no passado;
  • usar was e were corretamente;
  • descrever o que outras pessoas estavam fazendo.

Opening Dialogue

A:

"I called you at 8 PM yesterday, but you didn't answer."

B:

"Oh, sorry. I was having dinner with my family at that time."

A:

"Was your brother having dinner too, or was he doing something else?"

B:

"No, he wasn't eating. He was playing video games in his room."

Grammar: Past Continuous

Actions in Progress

Usamos o Past Continuous para descrever ações que estavam acontecendo em um momento específico no passado.

Structure

Subject + was/were + Verb-ing

  • I / He / She / It → was
  • You / We / They → were

Negative

Subject + wasn't/weren't + Verb-ing

"I wasn't sleeping at 10 AM."

"They weren't working."
