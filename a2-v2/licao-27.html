A good route has a clear order

First show the movement, then add one or two landmarks, and finish with the exact place.

How it sounds

Start: Go straight for one block.

Move: Turn right at the bakery.

Landmark: Walk past the park.

Arrival: The library is next to the cafe.

Say the route like a helper on the street: short, calm and easy to follow.

Tip: Use then and after that to connect steps.

Tip: Finish with a clear location clue.
