Grammar Refresher: Quick Review

Past Simple vs. Continuous

  • Simple: Completed actions.
    "I dropped the box."
  • Continuous: Actions in progress.
    "I was carrying the laptop."
  • Rule: Use While + Continuous and When + Simple for interruptions.
