Um turista perdido no seu bairro

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a localizar lugares e objetos com next to, between, in front of, behind, under e opposite, usando perguntas como Where is the bank?

Tema: mapa do bairro
Foco: localização
Situação real: ajudar alguém na rua

Você vai praticar

Descrever onde algo está
Responder Where is...?
Dar mini-direções com confiança

Tourist:

Excuse me, I'm a little lost. Where is the bank?

Student:

The bank is next to the supermarket, opposite the park. It is very easy to find.

Tourist:

Thank you. And is the pharmacy behind the bank?

Student:

No, it isn't. It is between the bakery and the bus stop, so you can stop there on the way.

Tourist:

Perfect. Is the park far from there?

Student:

No, it isn't. The park is right in front of the bank, so you will see it immediately.

How to locate places clearly

Perguntas úteis

QuestionMeaningExample answer
Where is the bank?Onde fica o banco?It is next to the bakery.
Where is the cat?Onde está o gato?It is under the table.
Is it behind the school?Está atrás da escola?Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

Como pensar

1. Ache o ponto principal.
Escolha o lugar de referência: bank, park, table, tree.
2. Escolha a posição.
Ex.: next to, behind, under.
3. Monte em inglês.
The pharmacy is between the bakery and the bus stop.

Erro comum

Em inglês, falamos next to the bank, não apenas next bank. A preposição precisa aparecer.

Dica prática

Quando a resposta fica longa, divida em duas pistas: It is next to the supermarket. It is opposite the park.

Homework: build your own neighborhood guide

Include in your homework

  • Draw or imagine a mini map with at least 5 places.
  • Write 6 to 8 sentences about that map.
  • Use at least 4 different position words.
  • Include one yes/no question and one short answer.
  • Prepare to explain the map orally next class.

Model

The bank is next to the bakery. The pharmacy is opposite the park. The bus stop is between the pharmacy and the supermarket. Is the school behind the bank? Yes, it is.
