Grammar: Prepositions of Place

Use prepositions to describe a location.

Next to (ao lado de)

"The bank is next to the supermarket."

Between (entre)

"The school is between the park and the bank."

Opposite (em frente, do outro lado)

"The park is opposite the bank."

On the corner (na esquina)

"The restaurant is on the corner."

Question:

"Excuse me, where is the bank?"

Use prepositions of place and include:

  • At least 3 places (e.g., a bank, a park).
  • At least 2 prepositions (e.g., "next to", "between").
  • Example: "On my street, there is a bank. The bank is next to a big park..."
