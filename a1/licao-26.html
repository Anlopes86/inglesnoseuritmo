Spoken review

Diálogo com descrição de ambiente

Progressão: o aluno já combina lugar, quantidade, habilidade e ação em andamento.

Teacher:

Look at this room. Is there a table next to the window?

Student:

Yes, there is.

Teacher:

How many books are there on the table?

Student:

There are three books on the table.

Teacher:

Can you see the boy in the picture? What is he doing?

Student:

Yes, I can. He is reading a book.

What Module 3 added

Places and quantity

Description tools

  • There is / There are
  • Prepositions like in, on, under, next to
  • How much / How many
  • A little / A few

Action and ability

Interactive language

  • Can / can't
  • am / is / are + verb-ing
  • Simple present vs. present continuous

Reading review: my neighborhood

There is a bakery next to my house, and there is a bank across from the bakery. There are a few restaurants on my street. Right now, my brother is walking to the supermarket. He can buy bread and milk there. I can walk to the supermarket in five minutes, so this neighborhood is very practical for my family.

Homework: prepare your module 3 checkpoint talk

What to include

  • 2 sentences with there is / there are about a room or neighborhood
  • 1 sentence with quantity using how much, how many, a little or a few
  • 1 sentence with can or can't
  • 2 sentences describing what people are doing now
  • Prepare 6 to 8 lines and be ready to speak them next class

Model checkpoint answer

There is a bakery near my house and there are two stores on my street. There is a little milk in my kitchen, but there aren't many apples today. I can cook simple meals, but I can't drive. Right now, my brother is studying in the living room and my mother is talking on the phone.

Dica: pense nesse homework como uma mini apresentação de revisão, não só uma lista de frases.
