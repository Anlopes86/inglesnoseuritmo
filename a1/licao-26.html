Grammar Review

A quick look at the main topics of this module.

  • There is / There are: Use to talk about existence. ("There is a book.")
  • Prepositions: Use to describe location (in, on, under, next to, between).
  • How much / How many: Ask about quantity for uncountable/countable nouns.
  • Can / Can't: Use for abilities, requests, and permission.
  • Present Continuous: Use for actions happening NOW (am/is/are + -ing).
  • Simple Present vs. Continuous: Routines vs. Actions happening now.

Homework: An Email to a Friend

Write a short email to a friend describing your city.

Try to include:

  • Something that there is/are in your city.
  • Something you can do there.
  • Something you are doing now.
  • Example: "Hi! How are you? I'm writing this email from my house. My city is great. There are a few nice parks here. You can walk and relax..."
