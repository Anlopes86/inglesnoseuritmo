Your Final Project: "My Life in 5 Minutes"

Prepare a short (2-3 minute) presentation about yourself. You can bring photos or just talk!

Your presentation should include:

  • Who you are: Your name, where you are from, your job.
  • Your routine: What do you usually do? (Simple Present)
  • Your last weekend: What did you do? (Simple Past)
  • Your future plans: What are you going to do next year? (Going to)
  • Your abilities: What is something you can do very well? (Can)
