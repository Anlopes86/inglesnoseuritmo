Articles and plurals

A vs. an

UseRuleExample
aantes de som de consoantea pen, a book, a wallet
anantes de som de vogalan apple, an eraser, an orange
Atenção: pense no som, não só na letra. Exemplo: a university.

Singular and plural

SingularPluralExample
one pentwo pensI have two pens.
one keythree keysThese are my keys.
one applefour applesThere are four apples.
