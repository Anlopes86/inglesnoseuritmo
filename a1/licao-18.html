Falar sobre profissões

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a perguntar e responder sobre profissões com estruturas como What do you do?, He is a doctor e She isn't a teacher.

Você vai praticar

Perguntar What do you do?
Responder com He/She is...
Falar do trabalho da família

Teacher:

What does your father do?

Student:

He is an engineer. He works for a car company.

Teacher:

Interesting. And what does your mother do?

Student:

She is a teacher. She works in a school and she loves her students.

Teacher:

And what about you? What do you want to do in the future?

Student:

I want to be a designer. I like creative work and technology.

Talking about jobs

Main questions

QuestionAnswerTradução

What does he do?He is a doctor.O que ele faz?

Useful patterns

He is a teacher.
Afirmativa simples sobre profissão.
He isn't a teacher. He is a doctor.
Negativa + correção.
Is she a chef?
Pergunta sim/não para confirmar profissão.

Quando usar

Use What do you do? para perguntar sobre a profissão da pessoa com quem você fala. Use What does he/she do? para perguntar sobre outra pessoa.

Dica importante

Para profissões, normalmente usamos a ou an: a doctor, a teacher, an engineer.

Erro comum

Depois de does, o verbo volta para a forma base: What does he do? e não What does he does?. Na resposta, usamos He is... ou She works....

Homework: build a job profile

Include in your homework

  • Name of the person.
  • Job title with a or an.
  • Where he or she works.
  • One thing the person likes or does every day.
  • Write 5 to 6 complete sentences.

Model

Her name is Paula. She is a nurse. She works in a hospital. She starts work early in the morning. She likes helping people. I think her job is important.

Dica: se quiser, escreva sobre um familiar real ou invente um personagem.
