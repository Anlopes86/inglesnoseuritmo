A1 - Lesson 14: How Often?

Warm-up: Your Week

Vamos falar sobre seus hábitos.

Professor: "Do you watch movies on the weekend?"

Aluno: "Yes, I sometimes watch movies."

Professor: "And do you study English every day?"

Aluno: "Yes, I always study English!"

Vocabulary: Adverbs of Frequency

Clique nos cartões para aprender a expressar frequência.

always (100%)

I always wake up early.

usually (80%)

I usually have coffee.

sometimes (50%)

I sometimes watch TV.

never (0%)

I never go to bed late.

Grammar: Position of Adverbs

O advérbio de frequência geralmente vem **antes** do verbo principal.

SubjectAdverbMain VerbRest of Sentence
Ialwaysstudyafter class.
Hesometimesplayssports.
Theyneverwatchhorror movies.

Pro Tip: The Verb "To Be" is Different!

Com o verbo "to be" (`am`, `is`, `are`), o advérbio vem **depois** do verbo.

Ex: "She is always happy." (NOT "She always is happy.")
