Diálogo-modelo

Uma conversa sobre hábitos

Teacher:

Do you have the same routine every day, or does it change during the week?

Student:

It changes a little. On weekdays, I usually study in the afternoon, but on Saturdays I sometimes study at night.

Teacher:

Interesting. Do you always drink coffee before you study?

Student:

No, not always. I usually drink coffee in the morning, but I never drink it late at night because then I can't sleep well.

Teacher:

And what about bedtime? Do you go to bed early?

Student:

During the week, yes, I usually go to bed before eleven. On weekends, I sometimes stay up late with my family.

Where does the adverb go?

With main verbs

PatternExample
subject + adverb + verbI always study at night.
subject + adverb + verbShe usually drinks coffee.

With verb to be

PatternExample
subject + be + adverbShe is always tired on Monday.
subject + be + adverbThey are never late.

Escala rápida

Always = 100%
Usually = quase sempre
Sometimes = às vezes
Never = 0%

Tradução

I never drink soda at night. = Eu nunca tomo refrigerante à noite.

Pergunta útil

Use How often...? quando quiser perguntar frequência: How often do you study English?

Erro comum

O advérbio geralmente vem antes do verbo principal: I usually study, não I study usually em frases básicas do A1.

1. I brush my teeth in the morning. Dica: sempre

Talk about your habits

Questions

  • How often do you study English?
  • How often do you drink coffee?
  • How often do you go to bed late?
  • How often do you watch TV at night?

Model

I usually study English in the evening. I always drink coffee in the morning. I sometimes watch TV at night, but I never sleep after midnight.

New dynamic

On weekdays, I usually...

Homework: compare weekdays and weekends

Include in your homework

  • Write 6 to 8 complete sentences.
  • Compare your weekdays and your weekends.
  • Use at least 4 frequency words.
  • Include one sentence about study, one about food or drink, and one about sleep.
  • Use but at least once to show contrast.

Model

On weekdays, I usually wake up early and study in the evening. I always drink coffee in the morning, but I never drink it at night. On weekends, I sometimes watch movies with my family and I usually go to bed late.

Dica: pense em rotina real, não em frases aleatórias.
