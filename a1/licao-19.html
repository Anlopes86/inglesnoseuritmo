Grammar: There is / There are

Usamos "There is" e "There are" para dizer que algo existe em um lugar.

1. Singular: There is (Forma curta: There's)

(+) Afirmativo: There is a bed in the bedroom.

(-) Negativo: There is not (isn't) a sofa in the kitchen.

(?) Pergunta: Is there a table?
→ Yes, there is. / No, there isn't.

2. Plural: There are

(+) Afirmativo: There are two chairs in the living room.

(-) Negativo: There are not (aren't) four windows.

(?) Pergunta: Are there two beds?
→ Yes, there are. / No, there aren't.

Homework: My Room

Write a short paragraph describing your bedroom or your dream house.

Use "There is" and "There are" and include:

  • At least 3 singular items (e.g., "There is a big bed.")
  • At least 1 plural item (e.g., "There are many books.")
  • At least 1 negative sentence (e.g., "There isn't a TV.")
