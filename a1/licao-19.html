Descrever a casa

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar there is e there are para descrever cômodos, móveis e objetos da casa.

Você vai praticar

Dizer There is a sofa
Dizer There are two chairs
Fazer perguntas com Is there...?

Teacher:

Tell me about your home. What rooms are there in your house or apartment?

Student:

There is a small kitchen, a living room, and one bedroom. There is also a bathroom near the bedroom.

Teacher:

Nice. Are there many things in the living room?

Student:

Yes, there are. There is a sofa, a table, and two chairs, but there isn't a television.

Teacher:

And is there a favorite room for you?

Student:

Yes. My favorite room is the bedroom because there is a desk there and I can study quietly.

There is and there are

Singular

FormExampleTradução
There isThere is a bed in the bedroom.Há uma cama no quarto.
Is there...?Is there a table?Tem uma mesa?
There isn'tThere isn't a TV here.Não há uma TV aqui.

Plural

FormExampleTradução
There areThere are two chairs.Há duas cadeiras.
Are there...?Are there two windows?Há duas janelas?
There aren'tThere aren't three rooms.Não há três cômodos.

Como escolher

Use there is com um item no singular. Use there are com dois ou mais itens.

Dica prática

Se você consegue contar one, use is. Se você consegue contar two, three, four, use are.

Describe your house

Use these prompts

  • There isn't a...
  • Is there a...?

Model

There is a kitchen and a living room in my house. There are two chairs in the kitchen. There isn't a sofa in my bedroom. Is there a TV in the living room? Yes, there is.

Homework: present your room or house

Include in your homework

  • Write 6 to 8 complete sentences.
  • Describe at least 2 rooms.
  • Use there is at least twice.
  • Use there are at least twice.
  • Include one negative sentence and one question.
  • Finish with your favorite room and why.

Model

There is a small kitchen in my house. There are two chairs in the dining area. There isn't a television in my bedroom. Is there a desk in my room? Yes, there is. My favorite room is the living room because it is comfortable.
