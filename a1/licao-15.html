A1 - Lesson 15: What Time Is It?

Warm-up: The Time Now

Vamos começar com uma pergunta simples.

Professor: "Excuse me, what time is it now?"

Aluno: "It's [hora atual]."

Professor: "Thank you! Are we late for the class?"

Aluno: "No, we are on time."

Vocabulary: Asking the Time

Clique para aprender diferentes formas de perguntar as horas.

What time is it?

(Informal)

What's the time?

(Informal)

Do you have the time?

(Comum)

Could you tell me the time, please?

(Formal / Educado)

Grammar: Telling the Time

Para responder, usamos **It's...**

9:00

It's nine o'clock.

2:30

It's half past two.

5:15

It's a quarter past five.

8:45

It's a quarter to nine.

Practice: What time is it?

Escreva as horas por extenso.

Homework: My Schedule

Escreva sua agenda diária com os horários.

Exemplo:

• I wake up at 7:00 (seven o'clock).

• I have lunch at 12:30 (half past twelve).

• My English class is at 6:15 (a quarter past six).
