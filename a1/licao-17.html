Grammar: Possessive 's

Usamos o Possessive 's para mostrar que algo pertence a alguém.

• The car of my uncle -> My uncle's car.

• The book of Maria -> Maria's book.

• The house of my parents -> My parents' house.

• The car of James -> James's car.

Spelling Rules:

• Para nomes no singular, adicione 's. (Ex: John -> John's)

• Para nomes no plural que terminam em -s, adicione apenas o apóstrofo '. (Ex: my parents -> my parents')

• Para nomes no singular que terminam em -s, o mais comum é adicionar 's. (Ex: James -> James's)

Dialogue: A Family Tree

Ouça e repita este diálogo sobre a família de Tom.

Alex: "Who is that? Is that your cousin?"

Tom: "Yes, that's my cousin, Anna."

Alex: "And who is that man? Is that Anna's father?"

Tom: "No, that's my uncle, Alex's dad."

Alex: "Oh, so that's your uncle, Mark."

Tom: "Exactly! And that's Mark's dog, Charlie."

Quiz Time: Whose Thing Is It?

Leia a descrição, tente adivinhar, e clique no botão para revelar a resposta.

Perguntas:

  • "This is my father's sister."
    Resposta: My aunt.
  • "This is my cousin's mother."
    Resposta: My aunt.
  • "This is my grandmother's son."
    Resposta: My father or my uncle.
  • "This is my friend's car."
    Resposta: Your friend's car.
