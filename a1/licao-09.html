Sua rotina diária em inglês

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a falar sobre hábitos com o simple present, descrever sequência do dia e usar verbos simples como wake up, have breakfast, go to work e go to bed.

Foco: hábitos

Você vai praticar

Falar da manhã ao fim do dia
Usar I / we / they no simple present
Montar mini-rotinas completas

Diálogo-modelo

Uma conversa real sobre o seu dia

Teacher:

Before we finish class, tell me about your day. What time do you usually wake up?

Student:

I usually wake up at seven o'clock. First, I brush my teeth and make coffee.

Teacher:

Nice. What do you do after breakfast?

Student:

After that, I take a shower and I go to work. I don't go by car. I go by bus.

Teacher:

Good. Do you study English every day?

Student:

No, I don't. I study on Tuesdays and Thursdays. At night, I have dinner and I go to bed at eleven.

Grammar: simple present for routines

Affirmative

SubjectPatternExample
Iverb baseI wake up early. = Eu acordo cedo.
Weverb baseWe have lunch at noon. = Nós almoçamos ao meio-dia.
Theyverb baseThey go to work by bus. = Eles vão ao trabalho de ônibus.

Negative

SubjectPatternExample
Idon't + verbI don't work on Sundays. = Eu não trabalho aos domingos.
Wedon't + verbWe don't study at night. = Nós não estudamos à noite.
Theydon't + verbThey don't go by car. = Eles não vão de carro.

Questions about routine

What time do you wake up? = A que horas você acorda?

Do you study at night? = Você estuda à noite?

What do you do after work? = O que você faz depois do trabalho?

Use order words

To organize a routine, use first, then, after that and finally.

Erro comum: com I / we / they, o verbo fica na forma base: I work, não I works.

Complete the routine

1. I at 7 AM. Dica: acordar

2. First, I at home. Dica: tomar café da manhã

3. After that, I . Dica: tomar banho

4. We at 8 o'clock. Dica: ir para o trabalho

5. They at noon. Dica: almoçar

6. I in the morning. I study at night. Dica: não estudo

7. At night, I at eleven. Dica: ir para a cama

8. On Saturdays, we early. Dica: não trabalhamos

Schedule interview

Use o quadro para entrevistar o colega ou responder sobre a sua rotina. A ideia é transformar verbos soltos em uma sequência mais real.

Ask these questions

  • What time do you wake up?
  • Do you have breakfast at home?
  • What do you do after work or class?
  • Do you study English every day?
  • What time do you go to bed?

Model answer

I wake up at six thirty. First, I make coffee. Then I go to work. I don't study in the morning, but I study English at night. I go to bed at eleven.

Homework: build your real daily routine

Write 7 to 8 lines

  • I wake up at...
  • Then I...
  • After that, I...
  • At noon, I...
  • At night, I...
  • Finally, I...
  • Add one negative sentence with don't.

Teacher-ready version

Bring this homework to the next class and be ready for 3 follow-up questions about your routine. If possible, record yourself reading it once.
