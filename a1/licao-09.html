A1 - Lesson 9: My Daily Routine

Warm-up: Your Morning

Let's talk about your morning routine.

Professor: "Good morning! How are you?"

Aluno: "Good morning! I am fine, thanks."

Professor: "Great. Tell me, what time do you usually wake up?"

Aluno: "I usually wake up at [7 o'clock]."

Professor: "And what is the first thing you do?"

Aluno: "First, I check my phone. Then, I have breakfast."

Grammar: Simple Present

Usamos o **Simple Present** para falar de hábitos e rotinas.

PronounAffirmative (+)Negative (-)
II work.I do not (don't) work.
YouYou study.You do not (don't) study.
WeWe live here.We do not (don't) live here.
TheyThey wake up early.They do not (don't) wake up early.

Explicação:

• Com I, You, We, They, o verbo fica na sua forma base (sem "to").
• Para a forma negativa, usamos do not ou a contração don't antes do verbo.
