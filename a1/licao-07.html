Apontar, identificar e responder

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar this, that, these e those para falar de coisas perto e longe, no singular e no plural.

Tema: objetos e espaço

Diálogo-modelo

Observe a conversa

Teacher:

What is this on your desk?

Student:

This is my notebook.

Teacher:

Great. And what is that near the door?

Student:

That is the teacher's bag.

Teacher:

Excellent. Are these your pens?

Student:

Yes, they are. But those books are not mine.

Questions

Use Is this...?, Is that...?, Are these...? and Are those...?

Quick tip

This / these ficam perto. That / those ficam longe.

Complete the sentences

1. is my phone. Dica: perto, singular

2. is the window. Dica: longe, singular

3. are my pencils. Dica: perto, plural

4. are your shoes. Dica: longe, plural

5. Are your glasses? Dica: perto, plural

6. Is your teacher? Dica: longe, singular
