A1 - Lesson 7: This or That?

Warm-up: Pointing Game

Vamos praticar apontar para objetos na sala.

Professor: (Segurando uma caneta) "What is this?"

Aluno: "This is a pen."

Professor: (Apontando para a porta) "And what is that?"

Aluno: "That is the door."

Vocabulary: Demonstrative Pronouns

Clique nos cartões para ver os exemplos.

This (Perto, Singular)

This is my book.

That (Longe, Singular)

That is your car.

These (Perto, Plural)

These are my keys.

Those (Longe, Plural)

Those are your books.

Grammar: This, That, These, Those

Vamos entender quando usar cada pronome.

Singular (um objeto)Plural (vários objetos)
Near (perto)This is an apple.These are apples.
Far (longe)That is a house.Those are houses.

Pro Tip:

Para fazer perguntas, a estrutura é a mesma do verbo "to be": inverta o pronome e o verbo.
• Is this your pen?
• Are those your keys?
