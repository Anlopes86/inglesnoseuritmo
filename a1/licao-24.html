Descrever o que está acontecendo agora

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar o present continuous para falar de ações em progresso, cenas do momento e situações temporárias.

Voc? vai praticar

Usar am / is / are + verb-ing
Perguntar What are you doing?
Descrever cenas do momento

Teacher:

Look around the classroom for a second. What are you doing right now?

Student:

I am listening to you, writing notes, and checking the examples on the screen.

Teacher:

Nice. And what is your classmate doing at the moment?

Student:

She is reading the slide and looking at her notebook, but she isn't speaking now.

Present continuous

Forms

TypeExampleTradução
affirmativeI am working now.Eu estou trabalhando agora.
negativeShe isn't sleeping.Ela não está dormindo.
questionWhat are they doing?O que eles estão fazendo?
Quando usar
Use para ações acontecendo agora, neste momento, hoje, ou em uma situação temporária.
Como montar
am / is / are + verbo com -ing: is reading, are listening.
Erro comum
Nao diga I reading. O verbo to be é obrigatório: I am reading.

Homework: describe a live scene for next class

Prepare to present orally too

  • Choose a real photo or imagine a clear scene
  • Write 6 sentences with at least 4 actions in progress
  • Include 1 negative sentence and 1 question
  • Mention where people or objects are in the room
  • Be ready to read it aloud in the next lesson

Model

Right now, I am studying in my room. My sister is listening to music near the window. My father is working in the kitchen. The TV is not playing, and my phone is charging on the table. My dog is sleeping under the chair. What am I doing? I am preparing for my English class.

Dica: imagine uma foto e descreva o que cada pessoa está fazendo.
