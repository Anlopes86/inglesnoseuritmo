Vocabulary: Small Quantities

These words are useful for talking about small amounts.

A few apples

Algumas maçãs (contável)

A little sugar

Um pouco de açúcar (incontável)

A few friends

Alguns amigos (contável)

A little time

Um pouco de tempo (incontável)

Grammar: A few vs. A little

Both mean "a small amount", but we use them differently.

A few (+ Countable Nouns)

We use a few for a small number of things we can count.

I have a few good friends.

There are a few apples in the kitchen.

A little (+ Uncountable Nouns)

We use a little for a small amount of things we cannot count.

I have a little money.

I need a little help, please.
