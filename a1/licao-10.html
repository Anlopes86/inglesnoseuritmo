A rotina dele e dela

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a falar sobre a rotina de outras pessoas usando he, she e o simple present com verbos como wakes up, goes, has e works.

Tema: rotina de outra pessoa
Foco: he / she
Sequência do dia

Você vai praticar

Descrever uma rotina com he / she
Usar verbos com -s corretamente
Fazer perguntas sobre hábitos diários

Diálogo-modelo

Observe a rotina da mãe do aluno

Teacher:

Tell me about your mother's routine. What time does she wake up?

Student:

She wakes up at five thirty. She has coffee and bread for breakfast.

Teacher:

Does she work in the morning?

Student:

Yes, she does. She goes to work at seven and comes home at five.

Teacher:

And what does she do at night?

Student:

She watches TV, talks to us and goes to bed at ten.

He / She no simple present

Forma afirmativa

SubjectPatternExampleTradução
heverb + sHe works at night.Ele trabalha à noite.
sheverb + sShe studies in the morning.Ela estuda de manhã.

Mudanças comuns

work → works
A maioria dos verbos só ganha -s.
go → goes
Verbos terminados em -o, -sh, -ch geralmente ganham -es.
have → has
É um caso irregular muito importante.

Atenção

Com I, you, we, they, usamos o verbo base: I work. Com he / she, o verbo muda: She works.

Erro comum

Não diga She work. Com she, o normal é She works. Já em perguntas com does, o verbo volta ao normal: Does she work?

Sound natural

Rotina em sequência

She wakes up early.

Then she has breakfast.

After that, she goes to work.

At night, she watches TV.

Mini strategy

Use then para continuar a sequência.

Use after that para conectar ações.

Use at night ou in the morning para dar contexto.

Talk about someone

Use these prompts

  • What time does he or she wake up?
  • What does he or she have for breakfast?
  • Does he or she work or study?
  • What does he or she do at night?

My father wakes up at six. He has coffee and fruit. He goes to work at seven. At night, he watches TV and goes to bed at ten.
