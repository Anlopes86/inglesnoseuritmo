A1 - Lesson 10: His/Her Routine

Warm-up: Family Routine

Vamos conversar sobre a rotina de alguém da sua família.

Professor: "Tell me about your mother or father. What time does he or she wake up?"

Aluno: "My mother wakes up at 6 AM."

Professor: "And does she have breakfast at home?"

Aluno: "Yes, she has breakfast at home."

Vocabulary Review: His/Her Routine

Vamos revisar as atividades, agora falando sobre "ele" ou "ela".

He wakes up.

Ele acorda.

She has breakfast.

Ela toma café da manhã.

He takes a shower.

Ele toma banho.

She goes to work.

Ela vai para o trabalho.

He studies English.

Ele estuda inglês.

She watches TV.

Ela assiste TV.

Grammar: Simple Present (He/She/It)

Para He, She, It, adicionamos **-s** ao final do verbo.

PronounAffirmative (+)
I / You / We / Theywork, study, go, watch
He / She / Itworks, studies, goes, watches

Spelling Rules (Regras de Escrita):

• Verbos terminados em -o, -sh, -ch, -x, -z: adicione -es (go -> goes, watch -> watches)

• Verbos terminados em consoante + y: troque o 'y' por -ies (study -> studies)

• Verbo 'have' é irregular: have -> has

Listening Activity

Ouça sobre a rotina de um ator famoso e responda.

Professor, dite o seguinte:

"This is Chris. He is an actor. He wakes up at 6 AM every day. He studies his scripts in the morning. He doesn't watch TV; he goes to the gym."

Aluno, responda:

1. What time does Chris wake up?

2. Does he watch TV?
