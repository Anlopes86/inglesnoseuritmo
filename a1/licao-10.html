Warm-up: Family Routine

Vamos conversar sobre a rotina de alguém da sua família. Clique em 🔊 para ouvir.

Professor: "Let's talk about someone in your family today. Tell me about your mother or your father — what does a typical day look like for them?"
Aluno: "My mother wakes up very early, at 5:30 AM. She works a lot."
Professor: "Wow, that's early! Does she have breakfast at home before she goes to work?"
Aluno: "Yes, she does. She always has coffee and bread. She doesn't eat a big breakfast."
Professor: "I see. And what does she do in the evening? Does she watch TV or does she go to bed early?"
Aluno: "She watches TV for about an hour, then she goes to bed. She usually sleeps at 10 PM."
Professor: "Perfect! You're already using He/She correctly with the verbs. That's exactly what we're going to practice today!"
💡 Observe: repare nos verbos — wakes up, has, doesn't eat, watches, goes, sleeps. Todos têm -s no final porque estamos falando de She. É isso que vamos estudar hoje!

Gramática

Grammar: Simple Present (He/She/It)

Para He, She, It, adicionamos -s ao final do verbo.

Pronoun Affirmative (+) Ouvir
I / You / We / They work, study, go, watch
He / She / It works, studies, goes, watches

Spelling Rules:

• Terminados em -o, -sh, -ch, -x, -z: adicione -es → goes, watches

• Consoante + y: troque por -ies → studies, tries

• Irregular: have → has
