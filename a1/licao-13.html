Quem recebe a ação?

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar me, you, him, her, us e them para falar de pessoas que recebem a ação.

Tema: relações
Foco: frases reais
Objeto direto

Você vai praticar

Dizer help me / call him / see them
Trocar nomes por pronomes
Escrever frases mais naturais

Teacher:

Who helps you with your homework when English is difficult?

Student:

My sister helps me with English, and my cousin sometimes explains new words to me.

Teacher:

Nice. And do you call your friends after class?

Student:

Yes, I call them every night, and I usually send them voice messages too.

Object pronouns

SubjectObjectExample
ImeShe helps me.
hehimI call him.
sheherWe know her.
weusThey help us.
theythemI see them.

Before / after

He helps me. The subject comes before the verb. The object comes after the verb.

Memory tip

If the person receives the action, use me, him, her, us or them.

Choose the object pronoun

1. She calls every day.

2. I know very well.

3. We visit on Sundays.

4. They help after class.

5. I see at school.

6. My teacher calls in the morning.

Homework: rewrite and expand with pronouns

Step 1

  • I call my mother. -> I call her.
  • She helps my brother and me. -> She helps us.
  • Do you know John? -> Do you know him?

Step 2

Create 4 new pairs of sentences from your life. First write the full noun, then rewrite with the pronoun.

Model: I see my friends at school. -> I see them at school.
