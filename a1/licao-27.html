Falar de onde as pessoas estavam

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende o passado do verbo to be: was / were, para falar de lugares, estados e informações pessoais no passado.

Você vai praticar

Usar was e were
Fazer perguntas no passado com was / were
Responder sobre onde alguém estava

Teacher:

Where are you now? Are you at home or somewhere else?

Student:

I am at home now, in my bedroom, because I am having class online.

Teacher:

Nice. And where were you yesterday afternoon? Were you at home too?

Student:

No, I wasn't. I was at work, and my parents were at home. At night, we were all together.

Past of to be: was / were

Main forms

SubjectFormExample
I / he / she / itwasI was tired.
you / we / theywereThey were happy.
negativewasn't / weren'tShe wasn't at school.
Perguntas
Was he at home? Were they at work?
Resposta curta
Yes, he was. / No, he wasn't. / Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.
Erro comum
No passado, não diga I am at home yesterday. Use I was at home yesterday.

Complete with was or were

1. I at the mall yesterday. Dica: eu estava

2. They late for class. Dica: eles estavam

3. She at home last night. Dica: não estava

4. We tired after lunch. Dica: não estávamos

5. he born in Brazil? Dica: ele nasceu...?

6. you at work yesterday morning? Dica: você estava...?

3. Carla was hungry. She was not at home.
4. Leo was with a teacher.

Homework: where were you yesterday?

Build a clear timeline

  • Write 6 to 7 sentences with morning, afternoon and night
  • Include 2 different places where you were
  • Say where another person was too
  • Include 1 negative sentence with wasn't or weren't
  • Finish with 1 feeling or opinion about the day

Model

Yesterday morning I was at work, but my sister wasn't there because she was at school. In the afternoon, I was at home for one hour. At night, I was at my aunt's house with my parents. We were a little tired, but we were happy because dinner was great.

Dica: use esse homework para treinar resposta longa quando o professor perguntar sobre ontem.
