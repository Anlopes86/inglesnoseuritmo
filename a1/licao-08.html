Review: Module 1

Agora você já consegue se apresentar, soletrar, falar de pessoas do seu mundo e descrever objetos muito básicos. Esta revisão junta essas habilidades no formato que o CEFR A1 espera: respostas curtas, claras e úteis.

Revisar: identidade e dados pessoais
Foco: speaking + form accuracy
8 etapas

Você precisa conseguir

Dizer nome, origem e informações básicas
Falar de família, amigos e objetos próximos
Responder perguntas simples com segurança

Spoken review

Conversa mais completa do que nas primeiras aulas

Progressão: aqui o diálogo já combina nome, origem, soletração e apresentação de outra pessoa.

Teacher:

Hello again. Let's review your basic profile. What is your full name?

Student:

My full name is Ana Souza.

Teacher:

Great. Where are you from, and how do you spell your last name?

Student:

I am from Brazil. S-O-U-Z-A.

Teacher:

Nice. Who is this with you in the photo, and what is this on the table?

Student:

This is my sister. Her name is Julia. This is her book.

What you should control now

Identity language

Core forms

  • I am / You are / He is / She is
  • My / your / his / her
  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?

Things around you

Reference words

  • This / that for one thing
  • These / those for more than one
  • Use nearby vs. far ideas
  • Describe simple classroom and home objects

Quick check

Complete com a palavra correta. Use o botão de revelar quando precisar destravar um item específico.

Choose the best answer

Essas perguntas simulam interações reais do começo do A1. Escolha a resposta mais útil e natural.

Teacher script: A new student

Teacher reads: “This is Lucas. He is from Mexico. His last name is Torres, T-O-R-R-E-S. This is his friend, Paula. She is from Brazil. They are students.”

Describe the picture

Prompt

Imagine a photo with a mother, a father, two children, a book and two school bags. Use these questions to speak or write:

  • Who is this?
  • Who are they?
  • What is this?
  • What are those?

Model support

This is the mother.

They are her children.

This is a book.

Those are school bags.

Homework: module 1 checkpoint

Junte tudo em um texto ou fala de 4 frases. Esse é um ótimo checkpoint do A1 inicial.

1. My name is ____.

2. I am from ____.

3. My phone number is ____.

4. This is my ____.

5. His/Her name is ____.

6. These are my ____ / Those are my ____.

Stretch goal

Turn the text into audio for the teacher. Speak slowly and clearly. If you cannot record, write one extra question you want to answer in class.
