Rotina ou momento atual?

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a comparar simple present e present continuous, entendendo a diferença entre hábito e ação em andamento.

Você vai praticar

Distinguir rotina e agora
Usar marcadores de tempo
Descrever contraste em uma frase

Teacher:

What are you doing right now, in this exact moment?

Student:

I am having an English class and taking notes on my computer.

Teacher:

Nice. Is that your usual Monday routine, or is today different?

Student:

Today is different. I usually work on Mondays, but today I am studying here because I have free time.

Simple present vs present continuous

Simple present

Use: hábitos, rotina, fatos.

Markers: usually, every day, on Mondays.

Example: I work every day.

Present continuous

Use: ações acontecendo agora.

Markers: now, right now, at the moment.

Example: I am working right now.

Comparação útil
I work on Mondays, but today I am studying English.
Como decidir
Pergunte: isso acontece sempre ou só agora?
Erro comum
Não use I am work every day. Rotina pede verbo simples: I work every day.

Choose the right tense

1. I every day. Dica: trabalho

2. Right now, I English. Dica: estou estudando

3. She coffee in the morning. Dica: geralmente bebe

4. They TV at the moment. Dica: estão assistindo

5. We soccer on Sundays. Dica: jogamos

6. He now because he is at lunch. Dica: não está trabalhando

Reading: today is different

Paula usually works in an office from Monday to Friday. She drinks coffee at eight and starts work at nine. Today is different: she is not working at the office. She is staying home, studying English, and talking to her classmates online.

1. Paula usually in an office. Dica: trabalha

2. She coffee at eight. Dica: bebe

3. Today she is home. Dica: ficando em

4. She is English. Dica: estudando

Homework: compare your routine and today

Turn this into a mini oral report

  • Write 7 sentences, not just 5 or 6
  • Include 3 routine sentences and 3 about today or now
  • Use at least 2 contrasts with but or today
  • Include markers like usually, every day, today, right now
  • Be ready to say the text without reading every word

Model

I usually work in the morning and study at night. I drink coffee every day, and I usually answer emails after lunch. Today is different. I am having an English class right now, and I am not working. I am studying grammar and practicing speaking because I have more free time today.

Dica: use palavras como usually, every day, today e right now.
