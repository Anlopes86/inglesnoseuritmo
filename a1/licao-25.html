Grammar: Simple Present vs. Continuous

Simple Present

USE:

  • Habits & Routines
  • Facts

TIME MARKERS:

usually, always, never, every day, on Sundays...

EXAMPLE:

"I work every day."

Present Continuous

USE:

  • Actions happening NOW
  • Temporary situations

TIME MARKERS:

now, right now, at the moment, today...

EXAMPLE:

"I am working right now."

Homework: A Postcard

Imagine you are on vacation. Write a short postcard to a friend.

Describe:

  • What you are doing now (Present Continuous).
  • What you usually do at home (Simple Present).
  • Example: "Hi! I'm having a great time. Right now, I'm sitting on the beach. At home, I usually work at this time!"
