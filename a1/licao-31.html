A1 Grammar Review

Let's remember the key topics.

Present

  • Verb to Be (am/is/are)
  • Simple Present (routines)
  • Present Continuous (now)

Past

  • Verb to Be (was/were)
  • Simple Past (Regular: -ed)
  • Questions & Negatives (Did/Didn't)

Future

  • Going to (plans)

Other Topics

  • There is/are
  • Can/Can't
  • Quantifiers (much/many, etc.)
