Teacher:

Let's do a final review. Tell me about your normal morning first. What do you usually do?

Student:

I usually wake up early, drink coffee, check my phone, and go to work.

Teacher:

Good. Now connect that with yesterday and tomorrow. What did you do yesterday, and what are you going to do tomorrow?

Student:

Yesterday I studied at home after work, and tomorrow I am going to visit my family and rest at night.

Your A1 review toolkit

Present

I work every day. / She doesn't like coffee. / They are at home.

Past

I watched a movie. / Did you study? / No, I didn't.

Future

I am going to travel. / She isn't going to work. / Are they going to cook?

Useful extras

There is / There are, can / can't, much / many, possessive 's.

Como revisar
Primeiro identifique o tempo: agora, ontem ou amanh?. Depois escolha a estrutura.
Erro comum
Misturar passado com base verbal errada: Did you watched? e I didn't went estão errados.
Meta da aula
Combinar frases simples de forma organizada, não usar tudo perfeitamente.

Homework: prepare your A1 mini profile

Include all three times

  • Write 7 to 8 sentences in a clear order
  • 1 sentence about who you are and where you are from
  • 2 sentences about your routine
  • 1 sentence about what you like or can do
  • 1 sentence about yesterday and 1 about tomorrow
  • Finish with 1 extra detail about family, house, neighborhood or goal

Model

My name is Diego and I am from Brazil. I work in a store and I study English at night. I usually drink coffee in the morning and talk to my mother after work. I like music, and I can cook simple meals. Yesterday I visited my cousin. Tomorrow I am going to study again and organize my room.
