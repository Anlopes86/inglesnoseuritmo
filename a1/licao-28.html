Contar o que aconteceu ontem

Nesta aula, o aluno começa a narrar ações no passado com verbos regulares, falando de rotina recente, passeios e pequenas histórias do dia anterior.

Você vai praticar

Usar verbos com -ed
Montar uma pequena historia de ontem
Ler e entender uma rotina no passado

Teacher:

Good morning. Yesterday was busy for me: I worked in the morning, cleaned the house in the afternoon, and visited my sister at night. How was your day?

Student:

My day was busy too. I studied English in the morning and watched a movie after dinner.

Teacher:

Nice. Did you stay home all day, or did you go somewhere in the afternoon?

Student:

No, I didn't stay home all day. I visited a friend in the afternoon, talked for two hours, and returned home before dinner.

Simple past with regular verbs

Main structure

FormExampleTradução
subject + verb-edI worked yesterday.Eu trabalhei ontem.
same for all subjectsShe visited her aunt.Ela visitou a tia dela.
verbs ending in ystudy -> studiedestudar -> estudou

How to think

Quando usar
Use para ações concluídas no passado: ontem, semana passada, no último fim de semana.
Palavras comuns
yesterday, last night, last weekend, in the morning, after dinner.
Erro comum
No afirmativo simples, não precisa de did: diga I watched, não I did watched.

Complete yesterday's story

1. Yesterday, I in the morning. Dica: trabalhei

2. After lunch, I my grandmother. Dica: visitei

3. We for an hour in the kitchen. Dica: conversamos

4. At home, I English for thirty minutes. Dica: estudei

5. At night, my sister and I a comedy. Dica: assistimos

6. Before bed, I my room. Dica: limpei

Reading: Clara's busy Saturday

Last Saturday, Clara stayed home in the morning and studied for a test. After lunch, she visited her cousin and talked about school. In the evening, they watched a movie and listened to music. Clara liked the day because it was simple but fun.

1. Clara for a test in the morning. Dica: estudou

2. After lunch, she her cousin. Dica: visitou

3. They a movie in the evening. Dica: assistiram

4. Clara the day because it was fun. Dica: gostou

Homework: tell yesterday as a short story

Make it sound like a real story

  • Write 6 to 8 sentences in time order
  • Include morning, afternoon and night
  • Use at least 4 different regular verbs in the past
  • Mention one person, one place and one feeling
  • Be ready to retell the story without reading line by line

Model

Yesterday I worked in the morning and finished at noon. After lunch, I visited my aunt and talked to her in the kitchen. Later, we watched a short series and laughed a lot. At night, I cleaned my room and studied English for thirty minutes. It was a busy but good day.

Dica: use conectores simples como after lunch, later e at night.
