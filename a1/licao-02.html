Gramática

Grammar: "to be" & "Where"

Vamos incluir He is (Ele é/ele está) e She is (Ela é/ela está).

Pronoun Affirmative (+) Negative (-) Interrogative (?)
He He is (He's) He isn't Is he?
She She is (She's) She isn't Is she?

Fazendo Perguntas com "Where":

• Use Where (Onde/Da onde) para perguntar sobre origem.

• Estrutura: Where + to be + pronoun + from?

Where is he from? Where is she from?
