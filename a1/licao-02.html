Fazendo Perguntas com "Where":

• Use Where para perguntar sobre lugares (origem).
• A estrutura é: Where + verb to be + pronoun.
• Exemplo: Where is he from?

Listening Activity

Ouça o professor e responda às perguntas.

Professor, leia as frases:

1. "Keanu Reeves is from Canada. He is Canadian."

2. "Penelope Cruz is not from Mexico. She is from Spain."

Aluno, responda:

1. Where is Keanu Reeves from?

2. Is Penelope Cruz from Mexico?
