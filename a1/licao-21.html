Preparando uma compra de mercado

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a diferenciar countable e uncountable nouns para perguntar quantidades com How many...? e How much...?.

Tema: compras
Foco: quantidades
Situação real: lista de mercado

Você vai praticar

Escolher how much ou how many
Separar contáveis e incontáveis
Falar da sua própria rotina de compras

Mother: We need food for the week, so let's organize the shopping list. How many apples do we need?

Student: We need six apples, because I want fruit for school and for breakfast.

Mother: Good idea. And how much milk do we need for the week?

Student: We need a lot of milk, maybe two bottles, because we drink coffee every morning.

Mother: Right. And what about eggs and rice? We need to think about lunch too.

Student: We need a dozen eggs and a lot of rice. There isn't much rice at home now.

How much or how many?

Countable nouns

UseQuestionTranslation
Itens que você contaHow many apples?Quantas maçãs?
PluralHow many eggs?Quantos ovos?

Uncountable nouns

UseQuestionTranslation
Substâncias ou ideiasHow much milk?Quanto leite?
Sem pluralHow much money?Quanto dinheiro?

Como decidir

Se você pode contar 1, 2, 3, use how many. Se pensa em quantidade geral, use how much.

Erro comum

Não diga how many milk. Milk é incontável, então use how much milk.

Choose the right question

1. bananas do we need? Dica: item contável

2. cheese is there in the fridge? Dica: alimento incontável

6. money do you have today? Dica: dinheiro

Homework: prepare a real shopping plan

Include in your homework

  • Write 6 to 8 lines.
  • Create 3 questions with how many.
  • Create 3 questions with how much.
  • Answer all of them as a real shopping plan for your home.
  • Include one sentence with a lot of and one with not much.
