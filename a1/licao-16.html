Spoken review

Conversa mais fluida

Progressão: aqui já juntamos rotina, preferência e follow-up questions.

Teacher:

What time do you usually wake up on weekdays?

Student:

I usually wake up at seven o'clock.

Teacher:

Nice. Do you study English every day or only sometimes?

Student:

I study English three times a week.

Teacher:

And what kind of music do you like?

Student:

I like pop music, but I don't like heavy metal.

Routine check

Complete com a forma correta. Misturei rotina, frequência e likes para a revisão ficar mais real.

Choose the best continuation

Aqui o foco é responder de forma mais natural, não só acertar gramática isolada.

Teacher script: Julia's week

Teacher reads: “Julia usually wakes up at six thirty. She works in the morning. On Tuesdays, she studies English at night. She likes pop music, but she doesn't like horror movies. On weekends, she always visits her grandmother.”

Build a short profile

Escreva ou fale 5 frases sobre uma pessoa. Esse é um ótimo treino antes de avançar para a próxima unidade.

Include these ideas

  • Name and job or role
  • Daily routine
  • One thing they like
  • One thing they don't like
  • One frequency expression

Model

This is my friend Caio. He works in a store. He usually wakes up at six. He likes action movies, but he doesn't like cooking. On Sundays, he always plays soccer.

Can you do this now?

By now, the learner can

  • Talk about routine with times and days
  • Say likes and dislikes clearly
  • Ask simple questions about habits
  • Use short follow-up answers

Watch out for

  • Third person -s
  • Do vs. does
  • Frequency words position
  • like vs. don't like
