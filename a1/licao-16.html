Como juntar tudo

Resposta boa no fim do Módulo 2 já mistura mais de uma ideia: I usually wake up at seven, I study English at night, and I don't like horror movies.

O que observar

Verifique sempre três pontos: horário, frequência e forma correta do verbo, principalmente com he / she.

Simple present em revisão

I work, mas she works. Quando você fala de he / she, normalmente o verbo ganha -s. Depois de doesn't, o verbo volta à forma base: She doesn't work at night.

Short answers e frequência

Em perguntas com do / does, use respostas curtas antes de expandir: Yes, I do. / No, he doesn't. Depois acrescente detalhe: No, he doesn't. He studies on Fridays.

Choose the best continuation

1. "Do you study English every day?" "No, ." Dica: resposta curta negativa

2. "What time does she wake up?" "She wakes up ." Dica: horário

3. "Does he like rock music?" "No, ." Dica: ele não

4. "How often do you go to the gym?" "I go on Saturdays." Dica: às vezes

5. "What do you do at night?" "I study and then ." Dica: dormir

6. "Does your mother like coffee?" "Yes, ." Dica: ela gosta

Reading: What Ana can say now

Ana is a student and she studies English. On weekdays, she usually wakes up at six thirty and goes to work in the morning. At night, she studies English three times a week. She likes pop music and action movies, but she doesn't like horror movies. On Saturdays, she sometimes goes to the gym, and on Sundays she always visits her grandmother. Build a short profile

Include these ideas

  • Name and role
  • Daily routine
  • One thing they like
  • One thing they don't like
  • One frequency expression

Model

This is my friend Caio. He works in a store. He usually wakes up at six. He likes action movies, but he doesn't like cooking. On Sundays, he always plays soccer.

Homework: module 2 speaking script

Prepare for the next class

  • Write 8 lines for a mini oral presentation.
  • Say what time you wake up and one part of your routine.
  • Use one frequency word.
  • Include one like and one dislike.
  • Write one Do you...? question for your teacher or partner.
  • Add one short answer: Yes, I do. or No, I don't.

Model

I usually wake up at seven. I study English at night three times a week. I like pop music, but I don't like horror movies. Do you study English every day? No, I don't. I study on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Dica: use este texto como roteiro para falar, não só para escrever.
