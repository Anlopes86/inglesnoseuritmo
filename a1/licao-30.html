Teacher:

You talked about yesterday last class. Now let's look ahead. What are you going to do tomorrow morning?

Student:

I am going to work in the morning, and I am going to study English at night.

Teacher:

Nice. Are you going to stay home all day, or are you going to go somewhere after lunch?

Student:

No, I am not going to stay home all day. I am going to visit a friend after lunch, and we are going to have coffee downtown.

Future with going to

Forms

TypeExampleTradução
affirmativeI am going to travel.Eu vou viajar.
negativeShe isn't going to work.Ela nao vai trabalhar.
questionAre you going to study?Voce vai estudar?
Quando usar
Use para planos e intenções já pensados: amanhã, semana que vem, depois da aula.
Como montar
am / is / are + going to + verbo base. O verbo principal não muda: going to visit, going to study.
Erro comum
Nao diga I going to travel. O verbo to be é obrigatório: I am going to travel.

Homework: prepare your weekend plan presentation

Include in your homework

  • Write 6 to 8 sentences about the next weekend
  • Include 2 plans for Saturday and 2 for Sunday
  • Mention 1 place, 1 person and 1 time expression
  • Add 1 negative plan with am not / isn't / aren't going to
  • Bring it ready for an oral mini presentation next class

Model

Next weekend, I am going to visit my cousin on Saturday morning. We are going to have lunch downtown and walk in the park in the afternoon. On Sunday, I am going to rest at home and study English for one hour. At night, I am going to call my grandmother. I am not going to work this weekend.
