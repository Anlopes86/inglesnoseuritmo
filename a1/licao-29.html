Past questions and negatives

Forms

TypeExampleTradução
questionDid you study?Voce estudou?
negativeI didn't study.Eu nao estudei.
short answerYes, I did. / No, I didn't.Sim, estudei. / Nao, nao estudei.
Regra principal
Depois de did ou didn't, o verbo volta para a forma base: Did you watch?, I didn't watch.
Erro comum
Nao diga didn't watched ou Did you studied?. O passado ja esta em did.
Dica de conversa
Primeiro faça a pergunta curta. Depois, acrescente detalhe: Did you study? Yes, I did. I studied at home.
