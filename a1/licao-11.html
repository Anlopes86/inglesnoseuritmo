A1 - Lesson 11: Do you like music?

Warm-up: Your Preferences

Let's talk about your likes!

Professor: "Tell me, do you like music?"

Aluno: "Yes, I do."

Professor: "Nice! And do you like to watch movies?"

Aluno: "Yes, I like movies too."

Professor: "Ok, great! Let's learn more."

Vocabulary: Hobbies & Interests

Clique nos cartões para aprender como falar sobre hobbies.

I like to listen to music.

Eu gosto de ouvir música.

He likes to watch movies.

Ele gosta de assistir filmes.

She plays sports.

Ela pratica esportes.

They read books.

Eles leem livros.

Grammar: Questions with DO and DOES

Para fazer perguntas no Simple Present, usamos Do e Does.

AuxiliaryPronounsExample Question
DoI / you / we / theyDo you like rock music?
Doeshe / she / itDoes he play sports?

Important!

• Quando usamos DOES, o verbo principal volta para a forma base (sem o "-s"). Ex: "He plays" -> "Does he play?"

• Para respostas curtas: "Yes, I do." ou "No, she doesn't."

Practice: Build the Question

Coloque as palavras na ordem correta para formar perguntas.

Speaking: Interview a Friend

Vamos praticar! Entreviste seu colega (ou o professor).

Aluno A: "Do you like to watch movies?"

Aluno B: "Yes, I do. / No, I don't."

Aluno A: "Does your mother like to read books?"

Aluno B: "Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't."
