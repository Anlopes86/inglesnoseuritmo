Gramática

Grammar: Questions with DO / DOES

Para fazer perguntas no Simple Present, usamos Do ou Does.

Auxiliar Pronomes Exemplo Ouvir
Do I / you / we / they Do you like rock music?
Does he / she / it Does he play sports?

✅ Respostas curtas afirmativas:

Yes, I do. / Yes, we do.
Yes, he does. / Yes, she does.

❌ Respostas curtas negativas:

No, I don't. / No, we don't.
No, he doesn't. / No, she doesn't.

⚠️ Important!

Com DOES, o verbo principal volta para a forma base (sem o "-s").

Does he plays? → Does he play? ✓
