Dizer do que você não gosta

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a usar don't like e doesn't like para falar de preferências negativas de forma simples e natural.

Tema: dislikes
Opiniões curtas
I / he / she

Você vai praticar

Dizer I don't like...
Dizer He/She doesn't like...
Comparar seus gostos com os da família

Diálogo-modelo

Gostos e desgostos do dia a dia

Teacher:

Let's compare opinions. Do you like horror movies?

Student:

No, I don't. I don't like horror movies because they are too scary.

Teacher:

What about your brother? Does he like them?

Student:

Yes, he does. He loves horror movies, but he doesn't like romantic films or slow series.

Teacher:

And what doesn't your mother like?

Student:

She doesn't like coffee. She likes tea, and she never drinks coffee at night.

Negative sentences with don't and doesn't

Main pattern

SubjectNegativeExample
I / you / we / theydon't + verbI don't like coffee. = Eu não gosto de café.
he / she / itdoesn't + verbShe doesn't like tea. = Ela não gosta de chá.

Important note

I don't like
Use com I, you, we, they.
He doesn't like
Use com he, she, it.
doesn't like, não doesn't likes
Depois de doesn't, o verbo volta à forma base.

Afirmativa → negativa

She likes jazz. → She doesn't like jazz.

Tradução

I don't like spicy food. = Eu não gosto de comida apimentada.

Homework: write a contrast profile

Write 6 to 8 lines

  • I don't like...
  • I like...
  • My mother/father doesn't like...
  • My brother/sister likes...
  • Use but at least twice.

Model

I don't like spicy food, but I like pasta. My sister likes tea, but she doesn't like coffee. My father likes soccer, but my mother doesn't.
