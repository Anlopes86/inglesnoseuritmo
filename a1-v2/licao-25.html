Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Can/could, must, should e imperativos

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
PermissionCan/Could + subject + verb?Can I take a taxi?Posso pegar um taxi?
AdviceSubject + should + verbYou should rest.Voce deveria descansar.
DirectionImperative verbGo straight. Turn right.Siga em frente. Vire a direita.
  • Could e mais educado que can em pedidos.
  • Must mostra obrigacao forte: You must stop.
  • Should/should not ajuda em conselhos de saude.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for walking in a park.

Situation 1

A:

Let's practice this situation: walking in a park.

B:

Do you have a pet?

A:

I have a dog.

B:

Cats are cute.

Situation 2

A:

Good. Now continue the same conversation.

B:

Birds can fly.

A:

There are trees in the park.

B:

The river is beautiful.

Situation 3

A:

One more short exchange, please.

B:

I like nature.

A:

We walk in the forest.

B:

That horse is big. Please protect animals.
