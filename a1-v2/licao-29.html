Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Simple present, present continuous e tempo

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
HabitSubject + present simpleI play tennis on weekends.Eu jogo tenis nos fins de semana.
NowSubject + be + verb-ingI am studying now.Eu estou estudando agora.
Future arrangementbe going to + verbWe are going to visit family.Nos vamos visitar a familia.
  • Use every day, usually e on weekends para rotina.
  • Use now, today ou at the moment para acoes acontecendo agora.
  • Adverbios geralmente ficam antes do verbo principal: I usually study.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for telling a short life story.

Situation 1

A:

Let's practice this situation: telling a short life story.

B:

Where were you born?

A:

I was born in Brazil.

B:

I studied English last year.

Situation 2

A:

Good. Now continue the same conversation.

B:

She worked in a school.

A:

We visited Rio.

B:

He moved to São Paulo.

Situation 3

A:

One more short exchange, please.

B:

I started a new job.

A:

They finished the course.

B:

Did you travel yesterday? No, I stayed home.
