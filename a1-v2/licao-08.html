Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Descricoes, adjetivos e direcoes

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
Adjectiveadjective + nouna tall man / a small houseum homem alto / uma casa pequena
DescriptionSubject + be + adjectiveShe is friendly.Ela e simpatica.
DirectionsImperative verb + placeTurn left. Go straight.Vire a esquerda. Siga em frente.
  • Em ingles, o adjetivo geralmente vem antes do substantivo.
  • Use preposicoes de lugar: next to, between, behind, near.
  • Para comparar duas coisas, use bigger than, smaller than ou more expensive than.

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for reviewing routine, time and food.
