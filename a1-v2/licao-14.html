Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Have, there is/are e obrigacoes

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
PossessionI/you/we/they + haveI have a computer.Eu tenho um computador.
ConditionSubject + have/has + symptomShe has a headache.Ela esta com dor de cabeca.
Placein / on / under / next toThe lamp is on the table.A lampada esta na mesa.
  • Use has com he, she e it.
  • Need to e have to indicam obrigacao: I need to study.
  • Em contexto de escola/trabalho, priorize clareza, nao frases longas.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for friends talking after class.

Situation 1

A:

Let's practice this situation: talking after class.

B:

What do you do in your free time?

A:

I like reading.

B:

I love cooking.

Situation 2

A:

Good. Now continue the same conversation.

B:

I hate dancing.

A:

I can play the guitar.

B:

I am into music.

Situation 3

A:

One more short exchange, please.

B:

I am keen on painting.

A:

I am not a fan of games.

B:

Do you like swimming? I usually watch movies.
