Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Qual é o problema?" and "Estou com dor de cabeça."
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Review e projeto final. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for reviewing health, home and work.
