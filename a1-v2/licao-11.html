Directions and Instructions

Nesta aula, o aluno pratica inglês A1 em uma situação real: a tourist asks for directions.

Focus: Places.

Cultural note

In English classes, short natural phrases are more useful than long translations. Use this topic (a tourist asks for directions) to compare one simple habit or situation in Brazil and in English-speaking contexts.

Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Descricoes, adjetivos e direcoes

Use be para descrever pessoas e lugares. Em ingles, o adjetivo normalmente vem antes do substantivo.

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
DescriptionSubject + be + adjectiveShe is tall.Ela e alta.
Adjective + nouna/an + adjective + nouna small houseuma casa pequena
DirectionImperative verb + placeTurn left. Go straight.Vire a esquerda. Siga em frente.
  • Use next to, between, behind e near para localizacao.
  • Imperativos comecam direto pelo verbo: Turn, Go, Cross.
  • Para comparar: bigger than, smaller than, more expensive than.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Com licença, você poderia me ajudar?" and "Como eu chego ao museu?"
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Descricoes, adjetivos e direcoes. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for a tourist asks for directions.
