Review 1

Focus: Review.

Cultural note

In English classes, short natural phrases are more useful than long translations. Use this topic (reviewing lessons 1 to 3) to compare one simple habit or situation in Brazil and in English-speaking contexts.

You will use

Nice to meet you.
Where are you from?
How old are you?
This is my sister.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Prazer em conhecer você." and "De onde você é?"
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Review e projeto final. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for reviewing lessons 1 to 3.
