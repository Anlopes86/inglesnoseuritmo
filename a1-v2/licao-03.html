Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Pronomes, to be e primeiras perguntas

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
AffirmativeSubject + be + complementI am Ana. / He is my brother.Eu sou Ana. / Ele e meu irmao.
NegativeSubject + be + not + complementI am not Robert. / She is not my sister.Eu nao sou Robert. / Ela nao e minha irma.
QuestionBe + subject + complement?Are you Bruno? / Is he your father?Voce e Bruno? / Ele e seu pai?
  • Use I, you, he, she, it, we e they para evitar repetir nomes.
  • I'm = I am, you're = you are, he's = he is. Em John's book, 's indica posse.
  • Para responder curto: Yes, I am. / No, he isn't.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for looking at family photos.

Let's practice this situation: looking at family photos.

B:

Situation 2

Good. Now continue the same conversation.

B:

I am an only child.

B:

Situation 3

B:
