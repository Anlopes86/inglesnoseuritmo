Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Rotina, present simple e preposicoes de tempo

Use present simple para rotinas e horarios. Use at para hora exata, in para partes do dia e on para dias.

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
RoutineSubject + verbI wake up at 7.Eu acordo as 7.
He/She/ItSubject + verb + -sShe starts work at 9.Ela comeca o trabalho as 9.
Timeat / in / onat noon / in the morning / on Mondayao meio-dia / de manha / na segunda
  • Com he, she e it, acrescente -s: he works, she studies.
  • Do/does aparecem em perguntas: Do you work? Does she study?
  • Adverbios como usually e sometimes ficam antes do verbo principal.

Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Que horas você levanta?" and "Eu acordo às sete."
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Rotina, present simple e preposicoes de tempo. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for talking before work.
