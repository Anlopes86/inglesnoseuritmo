Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Eu preciso de uma passagem." and "Onde fica a estação?"
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Review e projeto final. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for reviewing travel, holidays and sports.
