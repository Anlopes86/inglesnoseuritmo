Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Future: going to e will

Use going to para planos e will para previsoes ou decisoes feitas na hora.

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
Planbe going to + verbI am going to study.Eu vou estudar.
Predictionwill + verbIt will be sunny.Vai estar ensolarado.
QuestionWill + subject + verb?Will you travel?Voce vai viajar?
  • Going to combina com plano ja pensado.
  • Will combina com previsao ou decisao rapida.
  • Depois de will, use verbo base: will visit, nao will visits.

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "O que você vai fazer amanhã?" and "Eu vou estudar."
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Future: going to e will. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for planning next weekend.
