Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para revisar sozinho.

Simple rule

Planos, decisoes e comparacoes

UseStructureExamplePortuguês
Planbe going to + verbI am going to travel.Eu vou viajar.
Decisionwill + verbI will call you.Eu vou ligar para voce.
Comparisonadjective + -er / more + adjectivebigger / more expensivemaior / mais caro
  • Use going to para plano ja pensado e will para decisao ou previsao simples.
  • How much pergunta preco ou quantidade; how long pergunta duracao.
  • Superlativos usam the: the biggest, the most expensive.

Teacher guidance

  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Pause after each situation and ask: Who is speaking? What is the goal? Which phrase can change?
  • Have the learner repeat one line with natural intonation, then replace one word to personalize it for at a clinic.
