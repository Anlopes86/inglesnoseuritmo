Elaborated dialogue

Usage situations

Teacher guidance

  • Model natural intonation first; the learner repeats only one line at a time.
  • Assign roles A and B, then switch roles after the first reading.
  • Ask quick comprehension questions: "Eu tenho um cachorro." and "Pássaros conseguem voar."
  • Feedback focus for this lesson: Review e projeto final. Correct one priority, then let the learner personalize a line for reviewing nature, technology and culture.

Teacher feedback rubric

  • Vocabulary: the learner used lesson words in natural short phrases.
  • Meaning: the message is understandable, even with small errors.
  • Grammar focus: check Review e projeto final before correcting other grammar.
