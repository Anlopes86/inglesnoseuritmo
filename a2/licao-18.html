Grammar: Present Perfect

Life Experiences

Usamos o Present Perfect para falar de experiências passadas em um tempo indeterminado.

❓ Question: Have you ever + participle?

❌ Negative: I have never + participle.

✅ Positive: I have already + participle.

HAVE / HAS + VERB (3ª Coluna)

Task 1: Complete the Experiences

1. seen a ghost?

(Dica: Você já viu um fantasma?)

2. I to Paris.

(Dica: Eu nunca estive em Paris)

3. She done her homework.

(Dica: Ela já fez a lição)

4. They sushi.

(Dica: Eles nunca comeram sushi)

5. We this movie.

(Dica: Nós já vimos este filme)

6. lost his keys?

(Dica: Ele já perdeu as chaves?)

Task 2: Choose the Correct Grammar

"_______ you ever visited London?"

"I have ______ a famous actor."

"My dog ______ never bitten anyone."

"We ______ never heard that song."

"Have you ______ been to a farm?"

"She has ______ written a book."
