Lesson 10: Let’s Go Out!

Warm-up: Making Plans

Alex:

"Hey Sarah! Are you free this Saturday night?"

Sarah:

"I think so! Why? What are you going to do?"

Alex:

"I'm going to see a rock concert at the park. Do you want to come?"

Sarah:

"That sounds amazing! I'm gonna call my sister too. Is that okay?"

Alex:

"Sure! We are going to have a great time!"

Grammar: Formal vs. Informal

Going to

Usado em situações formais e na escrita correta.

"I am going to travel."

Gonna

Redução comum na fala rápida, músicas e filmes.

"I'm gonna travel."
Dica: "Gonna" já inclui o "to". Nunca diga "gonna to"!
