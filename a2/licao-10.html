A2 - Lesson 10: Will vs. Going To

1 / 7

Future choices

Compare plans, predictions and decisions in real situations.

Nesta aula, o aluno entende quando usar going to e quando usar will em contexto, não só em regra isolada.

Remember:

  • going to para planos ja organizados;
  • will para decisoes do momento e promessas;
  • o contexto da conversa ajuda a escolher a melhor forma.

Opening Dialogue

Nina:

"I already bought the tickets. We are going to visit Recife next month."

Leo:

"That sounds great. I think you will love the beaches there."

Nina:

"I still need a new suitcase, though."

Leo:

"No problem. I will help you choose one after class."

The Rules of the Game

Situation WILL GOING TO
Decisions Instant (Decidido agora) Pre-planned (Já decidido antes)
Predictions Opinions / Hunch (Acho que...) Evidence (Baseado no que vejo)
Special Use Promises & Offers Fixed Intentions
