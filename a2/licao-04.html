A2 - Lesson 04: Comparing Opinions

Warm-up: What Do You Think?

Let's share our opinions!

Teacher: "Which do you prefer, dogs or cats?"

Student: "I think dogs are more friendly than cats."

Teacher: "Interesting! I agree. But in my opinion, cats are quieter."

Student: "That's true. I think so too."

Grammar: Expressing Opinions

Giving Your Opinion

To express what you think, you can use these phrases:

  • I think pizza is delicious.
  • In my opinion, summer is better than winter.
  • For me, reading is more interesting than watching TV.

Agreeing and Disagreeing

Here's how you can respond to someone's opinion.

Agreeing

  • I agree.
  • I think so too.
  • That's true.

Disagreeing

  • I disagree.
  • I don't think so.
  • I'm not so sure.
