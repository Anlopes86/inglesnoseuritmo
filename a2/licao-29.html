Deal with shopping problems in English

New focus: receipts, sizes, polite complaints and clear return requests.

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a pedir outro tamanho, reclamar de um produto e falar de troca ou devolução com clareza.

You will use

It's too small.
Do you have this in a larger size?
I'd like to exchange it.
Bring the receipt and explain the problem politely.

Opening dialogue

Explain the problem and ask for a solution

Customer:

Hi. I bought this sweater yesterday, but it's too small.

Clerk:

No problem. Do you want to exchange it?

Customer:

Yes, please. Do you have it in a larger size?

Clerk:

Let me check. Do you still have the receipt?

In the store

Can I try this on?

Do you have this in a different color?

It's too expensive for me.

Use these before you buy or when you are still looking.

After buying

I'd like to exchange it.

I need a refund.

I bought it yesterday, but it's damaged.

Use these when the problem appears after the purchase.

Writing tip

1. Say what you bought

I bought this shirt yesterday.

I got these shoes last week.

2. Say the problem

It is too small.

It is damaged.

3. Say the solution

I would like a refund.

I would like to exchange it.

Use one simple sentence for each step and keep the tone polite.
