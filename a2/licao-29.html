Prompt set 1

Past story

Tell a short story about last weekend using 3 past verbs.

Comparison

Compare your city to another place you know.

Try to connect the two ideas with a transition like also, but or for example.

Prompt set 2

Experience

Say something you have done and something you have never done.

Advice

Give advice to someone with a headache and stress.

Use one full answer for each side, then try to connect them in one mini speech.

Speaking Builder

Connect vocabulary into one spoken answer
