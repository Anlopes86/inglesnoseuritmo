You will practice

Giving full route sequences

Checking understanding politely

Visitor: Can you help me get from the train station to the art gallery?

Local: Sure. Go straight for two blocks, turn left at the traffic lights, and walk past the park. The gallery is on your right, between the café and the bookstore.

Visitor: So I turn left after the station and walk past the park?

Local: Exactly.
