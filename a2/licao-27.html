Visitor: Can you help me get from the train station to the art gallery?

Local: Sure. Go straight for two blocks, turn left at the traffic lights, and walk past the park. The gallery is on your right, between the cafe and the bookstore.

Visitor: So I turn left after the station and walk past the park?

Local: Exactly.

A full route has 4 parts

A good route sounds natural when it moves from start to movement, then adds a landmark and finishes with a clear arrival point.

How it sounds

Start: Go straight for two blocks.

Move: Then turn left at the traffic lights.

Landmark: Walk past the park.

Arrival: The gallery is on your right.

Use short sentences, keep the order clear, and add one extra detail if the person still looks confused.

Tip: Start with the road, then the landmark.

Tip: Finish with the exact place.
