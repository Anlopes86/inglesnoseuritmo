Opening Dialogue

Friend A: "I'm feeling really stressed and tired lately. What should I do first?"

Friend B: "You should get some rest, and you shouldn't work all weekend without a break."

Friend A: "That makes sense. Should I also see a doctor if this continues?"

Friend B: "Yes, you should. It's important to take care of yourself before you feel even worse."

Grammar: Should & Shouldn't

Should (Conselho +)

Use para dizer que algo é uma boa ideia:

  • "You should drink more water."
  • "He should study for the test."

Shouldn't (Conselho -)

Use para dizer que algo NÃO é uma boa ideia:

  • "You shouldn't eat so much junk food."
  • "They shouldn't go out in the rain."
