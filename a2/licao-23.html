How imperatives work

Affirmative

Base verb only: Open the door.

Listen carefully.

Turn left here.

Negative

Don't + base verb: Don't open the window.

Don't run.

Don't touch that.

Instruction: Sit down.
Warning: Be careful.
Rule: Don't park here.

Common situations for imperatives

Classroom

Open your notebook.

Don't talk now.

Listen to the audio.

Street

Turn left here.

Wait at the corner.

Don't cross now.

Home

Close the window.

Put the keys here.

Don't leave the door open.

Emergency

Call the doctor.

Stay calm.

Don't move.

Choose affirmative or negative

1. It is a library. loudly. (dica: não fale)

2. The baby is sleeping. the door quietly. (dica: feche)

3. The light is red. now. (dica: não atravesse)

4. The teacher is speaking. carefully. (dica: escute)

5. It is dangerous here. in the street. (dica: não pare)
