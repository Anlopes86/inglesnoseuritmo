Ask for help in the street with confidence

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a pedir informações de caminho com educação, entender respostas curtas e localizar pontos importantes da cidade.

Tema: asking for directions
Foco: street interaction
CEFR: survival English

You will practice

Using Excuse me and How do I get to...?
Understanding places like next to and across from
Asking one follow-up question naturally

Street dialogue

Tourist:

Excuse me, how do I get to the city museum?

Local:

Go straight for two blocks. The museum is next to the bank.

Tourist:

Is it far from here?

Local:

No, not really. It is across from the park, on the corner.

Polite questions + place relations

Useful question pattern

Excuse me, how do I get to the station?

Também é comum usar:

Where is the pharmacy?

Is it far from here?

Place prepositions

next to = ao lado

across from = em frente

on the corner = na esquina

between = entre dois lugares

Model: Excuse me, how do I get to the bank? It is next to the supermarket.
Watch out: em perguntas com do, o verbo volta à forma base: How do I get.
