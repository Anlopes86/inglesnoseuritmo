Grammar focus

Understand the pattern

Exemplos com tradução para o aluno conseguir revisar sozinho.

Advice with Should

Use should + verbo base para dar conselho ou recomendação.

People should recycle paper.

As pessoas deveriam reciclar papel.

We should use less plastic.

Nós deveríamos usar menos plástico.

You should turn off the lights.

Você deveria apagar as luzes.

Obligation with Must / Have To

Use must ou have to para obrigação, necessidade forte ou regra importante.

We must protect the planet.

Nós devemos proteger o planeta.

Companies have to reduce waste.

Empresas precisam reduzir desperdício.

People must not throw bags in the street.

As pessoas não devem jogar sacolas na rua.

Passive Voice

Use is/are + past participle para focar no processo, não em quem faz a ação.

Paper is recycled at school.

O papel é reciclado na escola.

Plastic bottles should be reused.

Garrafas plásticas deveriam ser reutilizadas.

Glass is collected every Friday.

O vidro é coletado toda sexta-feira.

Grammar of expressions

Environmental grammar patterns

Use estes padrões para dar conselhos, falar de regras e comparar quantidades.

Advice and Rules: modal + base verb

Depois de must, should e have to, use o verbo base, sem to.

We must protect the planet.

Nós devemos proteger o planeta.

People should recycle paper.

As pessoas deveriam reciclar papel.

Companies have to reduce waste.

As empresas precisam reduzir o desperdício.

Modal Passive: modal + be + participle

Use este padrão quando o foco é o que deve ser feito com o objeto, e não quem faz a ação.

Plastic should be recycled.

O plástico deveria ser reciclado.

Old boxes can be reused.

Caixas antigas podem ser reutilizadas.

Water must not be wasted.

A água não deve ser desperdiçada.

Quantity: less x fewer

Use less com coisas incontáveis e fewer com coisas contáveis no plural.

We should use less plastic.

Nós deveríamos usar menos plástico.

People should use fewer bags.

As pessoas deveriam usar menos sacolas.

The city needs less pollution.

A cidade precisa de menos poluição.

Reading: A Greener School

Leitura guiada com modals, passive voice e vocabulario ambiental.

Text

At Green Valley School, students are worried about the amount of waste they produce every day. Plastic bottles are thrown away after lunch, paper is used only once, and too much water is wasted in the bathrooms.

The student council believes the school must change its habits. More paper should be recycled, fewer plastic bags should be used, and lights have to be turned off when classrooms are empty. They also want to place recycling bins near the cafeteria.

The principal agrees with the plan. Next month, glass and plastic will be collected every Friday, and a short environmental message will be shared during the morning announcements. The students hope their school can use less plastic and save more energy.

Comprehension

1. What problems do students notice at school?

2. What should be recycled?

3. When will glass and plastic be collected?

Language focus

Find two sentences with should or must.

Find two examples of the passive voice.

Create one sentence with less and one with fewer.
