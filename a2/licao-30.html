Opening dialogue

Invite, suggest and confirm

Lia:

Are you free on Saturday afternoon?

Dan:

I think so. What time do you want to meet?

Lia:

Let's meet at 3 p.m. at the coffee shop near the station.

Dan:

Sounds good. If I'm late, I'll text you.

Inviting and planning

Are you free tonight?

Do you want to grab coffee?

Let's meet in front of the mall.

Use these to start the conversation in a friendly way.

Changing and confirming

Can we change the time?

I'm going to be a little late.

So we're meeting at 6, right?

Use these to keep the plan clear when something changes.

Roleplay: making plans

Student A

Invite your partner to do something this weekend. Suggest a time and place.

Student B

Accept, refuse politely or ask to change the time. Then confirm the final plan.

Do one round quickly, then a second round with a change so the conversation feels real.
