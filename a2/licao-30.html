Arrange plans and confirm details

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a convidar alguém, combinar horário e lugar, remarcar e confirmar planos com naturalidade.

You will use

Are you free on Friday?
Let's meet at 7.
Can we change the time?

Opening dialogue

Lia: Are you free on Saturday afternoon?

Dan: I think so. What time do you want to meet?

Lia: Let's meet at 3 p.m. at the coffee shop near the station.

Dan: Sounds good. If I'm late, I'll text you.

Note como a conversa mistura disponibilidade, horário, lugar e confirmação em poucas falas.

Inviting and planning

Are you free tonight?

Do you want to grab coffee?

Let's meet in front of the mall.

Changing and confirming

Can we change the time?

I'm going to be a little late.

So we're meeting at 6, right?

Arrangement Builder

Build a full plan

O aluno monta um convite completo com dia, lugar e confirmação, em vez de praticar frases soltas.

1. Invite

2. Place and time

3. Confirm or change

Leia como uma conversa real entre amigos ou colegas.

Roleplay: making plans

Student A

Invite your partner to do something this weekend. Suggest a time and place.

Student B

Accept, refuse politely or ask to change the time. Then confirm the final plan.

Homework

Write a short message exchange in 6 to 8 lines. Invite someone, suggest a place and time, then add one change or confirmation.
