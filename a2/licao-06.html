A2 - Lesson 06: Setting the Scene

Warm-up: At 8 PM Yesterday

O que estava acontecendo?

A: "I called you at 8 PM yesterday, but you didn't answer."

B: "Oh, sorry! I was having dinner with my family."

A: "Was your brother having dinner too?"

B: "No, he wasn't eating. He was playing video games in his room."

Grammar: Past Continuous

Actions in Progress

Usamos o Past Continuous para descrever ações que estavam acontecendo em um momento específico no passado.

Structure

Subject + was/were + Verb-ing

  • I / He / She / It → was
  • You / We / They → were

Negative

Subject + wasn't/weren't + Verb-ing

"I wasn't sleeping at 10 AM."

"They weren't working."
