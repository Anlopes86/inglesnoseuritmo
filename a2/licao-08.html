Task 1: Verb Tenses & Comparatives

2. Traveling by train is (relaxing) flying.

3. She (not go) to work last Monday.

4. My city is (noisy) your town.

5. While he (cook), he cut his finger.

6. Is your house (as/big) mine?

Task 3: Correct the Mistakes

Identify the error and type the correct sentence:

"She is more happy than her sister."

"I were eating when you called."

"Math is badder than History."

"They didn't saw the movie."

Task 4: Logic Challenge

Rewrite using comparatives (-er / more than):

1. The bike isn't as fast as the car.
→ The car is the bike.

2. I'm not as tall as my brother.
→ My brother is me.

3. This cake isn't as delicious as that one.
→ That cake is this one.

4. Soccer isn't as dangerous as rugby.
→ Rugby is soccer.
