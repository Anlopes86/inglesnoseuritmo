Lesson 14: Do I Have to...?

Warm-up: Travel Questions

Traveler:

"Excuse me, do I have to show my passport now?"

Officer:

"Yes, you must show it at the gate. And you mustn't leave your bags alone."

Traveler:

"Understood. Does my friend have to take off his shoes?"

Officer:

"No, he doesn't have to, but he must put all metal objects in the tray."

Grammar Focus: Questions & Must

Interrogative

Use Do ou Does para perguntar sobre obrigações:

  • "Do you have to pay?"
  • "Does he have to wait?"

Must & Mustn't

Must: Obrigação forte/regra.

Mustn't: Proibição absoluta.

  • "You must wear a belt."
  • "You mustn't park here."

3. Visitors be quiet. (regra essencial)

4. You feed the animals. (proibição total)

5. All passengers wear seatbelts. (obrigação legal)
