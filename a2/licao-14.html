Opening Dialogue

Traveler: "Excuse me, do I have to show my passport now or only at the gate?"

Officer: "You must show it now, and you mustn't leave your bags alone at any time."

Traveler: "Understood. Does my friend have to take off his shoes too?"

Officer: "He doesn't have to, but he must put all metal objects in the tray before entering."

Grammar Focus: Questions & Must

Interrogative

Use Do ou Does para perguntar sobre obrigações:

  • "Do you have to pay?"
  • "Does he have to wait?"

Must & Mustn't

Must: Obrigação forte/regra.

Mustn't: Proibição absoluta.

  • "You must wear a belt."
  • "You mustn't park here."
