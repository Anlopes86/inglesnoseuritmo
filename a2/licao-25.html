Why it works

Imperatives são diretos, curtos e naturais para instruções. Você pode ligar etapas com and ou then.

Complete the direction

1. straight for one block.

2. Turn at the supermarket.

3. the street and stop at the corner.

4. The station is the bank.

5. It is the bakery.
