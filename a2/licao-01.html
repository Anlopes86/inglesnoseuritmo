Grammar: The Simple Past

Using the Simple Past

We use the Simple Past to talk about completed actions in the past. The action is finished.

Regular Verbs

For regular verbs, we add -ed to the base form.

  • work → worked
  • study → studied
  • watch → watched

Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs have a special past form that you need to memorize.

  • go → went
  • have → had
  • eat → ate

Sentence Structure

For ALL verbs (regular and irregular), the structure for negatives and questions is the same.

Form Structure Example
(+)Subject + Verb in PastShe worked. / She went.
(-)Subject + didn't + Verb (base)She didn't work. / She didn't go.
(?)Did + Subject + Verb (base)?Did she work? / Did she go?
