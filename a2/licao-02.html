A2 - Lesson 02: Telling a Story

Warm-up: Your Last Trip

Think about the last trip you took. Where did you go?

Teacher: "Tell me about your last trip. Did you go to the beach or the mountains?"

Student: "I went to the beach. I traveled to Rio de Janeiro."

Teacher: "Oh, nice! What did you do there?"

Student: "I visited many places and ate great food."

Grammar: Sequence Connectors

How to Tell a Story in Order

To connect ideas and show the order of events, we use sequence connectors.

First, I packed my bags.

Then, I took a taxi to the airport.

After that, I flew to New York.

Finally, I arrived at my hotel.

More Irregular Verbs

Let's learn some new irregular verbs common in stories.

  • take → took
  • see → saw
  • buy → bought
  • think → thought
  • feel → felt
  • find → found
