A2 - Lesson 02: Telling a Story

Warm-up: A Short Trip

Let's talk about a recent event!

Teacher: "I went to the beach last weekend! First, I packed my bags. Then, I drove for two hours. Finally, I arrived!"

Teacher: "What about you? Did you travel recently?"

Student: "Yes, I went to [city] last month."

Teacher: "Awesome! What did you do there?"

Student: "First, I visited a museum. Then, I ate at a nice restaurant."

Grammar: More Irregular Verbs & Connectors

More Common Irregular Verbs

Remember, irregular verbs don't follow the "-ed" rule. Here are more important ones for telling stories:

see → saw

buy → bought

take → took

find → found

Sequence Connectors

Use these words to connect your ideas and tell a story in chronological order.

First, I woke up early.

Then, I had breakfast.

After that, I went to work.

Finally, I came back home.
