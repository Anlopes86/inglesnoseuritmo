Reading Challenge: The CEO's Routine

Mr. Thompson is the richest businessman in the city, but he has the busiest schedule too. He always says: "To be successful, you have to wake up early and you shouldn't waste your time." Next week, he is going to fly to Tokyo for the most important meeting of the year. He must sign a new contract, or his company will lose money. His doctor told him: "You are very stressed. You should take a vacation soon." Mr. Thompson is going to think about it. He doesn't have to work on Sundays, but he usually does.

1. Why is his schedule difficult?

2. What advice did the doctor give?

3. Is working on Sunday an obligation?

4. Where is he going next week?
