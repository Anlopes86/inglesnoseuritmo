Grammar: The "Future Plan" Formula

Usage:

  • Plans & Intentions: "I'm going to study tonight."
  • Predictions (Evidence): "Look at the clouds! It's going to rain."

Structure:

Subject + BE + GOING TO + VERB

Task 1: Build the Sentences

1. (I / visit) my family.

2. (It / not / be) sunny tomorrow.

3. (They / have) a party.

4. (? / you / buy) a new car?

5. (We / play) soccer if it's clear.

Task 2: Predict the Weather

1. The thermometer says -5°C!

2. There is no sun and many gray clouds.

3. The trees are moving a lot!

4. I can hear thunder (trovão).

Task 3: Fix the Future

"I going to travel next week."

"Is going to rain tomorrow?"

"They are go to buy a house."
