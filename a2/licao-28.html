Handle a hotel stay in English

Nesta aula, o aluno aprende a fazer check-in, pedir ajuda, relatar problemas no quarto e resolver situações comuns de hospedagem.

You will use

I have a reservation.
Could I have another towel?
The shower doesn't work.

Opening dialogue

Receptionist: Good evening. Welcome to the Blue River Hotel. How can I help you?

Guest: Hi. I have a reservation under Carla Mendes.

Receptionist: Great. You're in room 304. Here is your key card.

Guest: Thank you. One more thing: could I have an extra towel?

Observe como o diálogo mistura identificação, pedido simples e vocabulário de hotel sem ficar artificial.

At check-in

I have a reservation.

I booked a double room.

What time is breakfast?

Problems and requests

The air conditioner isn't working.

Could I have another pillow?

Can someone help me with my bags?
