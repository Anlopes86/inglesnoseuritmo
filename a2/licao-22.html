Lesson 22: Clinical Advice

Clinical Case Study

MD:

"What is your chief complaint today?"

Patient:

"I've been feeling short of breath and I have a sharp pain in my chest."

MD:

"On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate the pain? You should describe it precisely."

MD (Advice):

"You shouldn't ignore these symptoms. We should perform an EKG immediately."
