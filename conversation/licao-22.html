Lesson 22: Art & Creativity

Warm-up: Creative Minds

Let's start our conversation:

Do you consider yourself a creative person? Why or why not?

What is your favorite form of art (painting, music, cinema, etc.)?

Does art need to have a meaning, or can it just be beautiful?

If you could meet any artist (past or present), who would it be?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore artistic concepts.

Masterpiece

A work of outstanding artistry, skill, or workmanship.

Think out of the box

To think differently, unconventionally, or from a new perspective.

Aesthetics

A set of principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty.

To spark (creativity)

To ignite or provide the initial stimulus for a creative process.

Exhibition

A public display of works of art or items of interest.

State-of-the-art

Using the newest and most advanced ideas or features.
