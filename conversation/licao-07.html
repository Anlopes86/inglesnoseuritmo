Lesson 7: Travel & Adventure

Warm-up: Let's Travel

Let's start our conversation:

What’s the most interesting place you’ve visited?

If you could go anywhere tomorrow, where would you go?

Do you prefer traveling alone or with others?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about travel.

To hit the road

To begin a journey or a trip.

Wanderlust

A strong desire to travel and explore the world.

Off the beaten path

A place that is not well-known or popular with tourists.

A change of scenery

Moving to a different place to have a new environment.

To travel light

To travel with very little luggage.

A once-in-a-lifetime trip

A special trip that you will probably only take once.
