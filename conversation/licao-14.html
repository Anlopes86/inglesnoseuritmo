Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about food.

A foodie

A person who loves and is very interested in food.

To have a sweet tooth

To have a strong liking for sweet foods.

A picky eater

Someone who is very selective about what they eat.

Comfort food

Food that provides a feeling of well-being.

To wine and dine

To entertain someone with a fancy meal.

My mouth is watering

Said when you see or smell delicious food.
