Key Expressions

Soundtrack of your life

The songs that represent important parts of your life.

To hit home

To affect you deeply because it feels personally true.

To get goosebumps

To have a strong emotional or physical reaction to music.

Mood booster

Something that quickly improves your mood.

To relate to

To feel a personal connection with lyrics or emotions.

To put feelings into words

To express emotions clearly through language or lyrics.

Why Music Feels So Personal

Music can shape identity.

The songs we choose often reflect our emotions, values, social groups, memories, and even the image we want to project to others.

Emotion: music helps us feel understood, energized, calm, or nostalgic.
Identity: music taste can signal personality, culture, age, and belonging.

Do we choose music, or does music help shape who we become?
