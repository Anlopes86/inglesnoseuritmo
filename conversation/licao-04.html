Lesson 4: City Life vs. Country Life

Warm-up: Your Preference

Let's start our discussion:

Do you prefer living in a big city or in the countryside? Why?

What are the pros and cons of city life?

Describe your dream place to live.

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore the ideas of this lesson.

The hustle and bustle

The busy and noisy activity of a city.

Peace and quiet

A calm and tranquil environment.

Cost of living

The amount of money needed to live in a certain place.

Pace of life

The speed at which life is lived.

Concrete jungle

A term for a big city with many buildings.

To get away from it all

To take a vacation to escape a stressful life.
