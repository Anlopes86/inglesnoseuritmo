Warm-up: Words and Meaning

Let's talk about speaking, understanding, and misunderstanding.

What makes communication difficult even when people speak the same language?

Is speaking a language well more about grammar or confidence?

Have you ever been completely misunderstood?

Is listening harder than speaking?

Key Expressions

To get your point across

To communicate your idea clearly and effectively.

Mixed signals

Messages that are confusing or contradictory.

Small talk

Light, casual conversation about simple topics.

On the same wavelength

Thinking and communicating in a similar way.

To read between the lines

To understand an implied meaning not stated directly.

Lost in translation

Something whose meaning is weakened or changed when translated.

Beyond Grammar

Communication is more than words.

Tone, timing, body language, culture, and assumptions all shape meaning. Many conflicts happen because people hear a message differently from how it was intended.

Speaking: clarity, confidence, structure, empathy.
Listening: patience, attention, interpretation, openness.

Is communication mainly about expressing yourself, or about being understood?
