Useful Expressions

Click the cards to explore expressions about sports and competition.

To be a couch potato

To be a lazy person who spends a lot of time watching TV.

To get the ball rolling

To start a process or activity.

To be neck and neck

When two competitors are very close in a race or contest.

To go the extra mile

To make a special, extra effort to achieve something.

To be a good sport

To be a person who can accept losing in a good-natured way.

To throw in the towel

To give up on something; to quit.
