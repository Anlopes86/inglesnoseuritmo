Fashion Vocabulary

Trend

A general direction in which something is developing or changing; what's "in" right now.

Vintage

Clothing from a previous era, typically at least 20 years old.

Sustainable

Eco-friendly fashion that focuses on ethical production and longevity.

Outfit

A set of clothes worn together, typically for a particular occasion.

Tailored

Clothing made or adjusted to fit a specific person perfectly.

Thrifting

The act of shopping at second-hand stores to find unique or cheaper items.

Sustainable Fashion

The "Thrift Shop" approach is better for the planet. Do you ever buy second-hand clothes?

What do you think of "Fast Fashion" (brands like Shein or Zara)? Is it ethical to buy cheap clothes that don't last?

How many years do you usually keep an item of clothing before getting rid of it?

The Psychology of Color

The colors we wear can influence our mood and how others perceive us. Let's discuss:

Red

Energy, passion, power. How do you feel when wearing red?

Blue

Trust, calm, professional. Is this your "go-to" color for work?

Yellow

Happiness, optimism, attention. Would you wear a full yellow outfit?

Black

Elegance, mystery, authority. Is black the most versatile color?
