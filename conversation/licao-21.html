Lesson 21: Volunteering & Social Causes

Warm-up: About Social Causes

Let's start our conversation:

Have you ever volunteered for a cause? If so, what was it?

What social cause are you most passionate about?

Do you think companies have a social responsibility to give back?

What's the most effective way to help people in need?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about helping others.

To give back

To help people or a community, especially because they have helped you.

Charity

An organization that helps people in need.

To make a difference

To have a positive impact.

Fundraising

The act of collecting money for a cause.

Social issue

A problem that affects a large group of people in a society.

Grassroots movement

A social or political movement that begins on a local level.

Homework

Write a short paragraph about a social cause you care about. Explain why it is important and what you could do to help.

Example: "A social cause that is very important to me is animal welfare. It's a problem because many animals are abandoned and need help. I could volunteer at an animal shelter to help feed them and play with them. Every little bit of help can make a difference in their lives."
