What Makes Love Last?

Love is not only chemistry.

Attraction may bring two people together, but respect, trust, and communication are often what keep a relationship alive over time.

Strong relationship: honesty, support, emotional safety, shared effort.
Weak relationship: jealousy, silence, manipulation, lack of respect.

Can love survive without trust?
