Useful Expressions

Click on the cards to flip them and see the meaning.

To fall for someone

To fall in love with someone.

"He fell for her the moment they met."

Head over heels

To be completely in love.

"They are head over heels for each other."

To tie the knot

To get married.

"They're planning to tie the knot next year."

To have a crush on

A secret romantic feeling for someone.

"I had a huge crush on my history teacher."

To pop the question

To ask someone to marry you.

"He popped the question on her birthday."

A match made in heaven

Two people who are perfect for each other.

"Everyone agrees they are a match made in heaven."
