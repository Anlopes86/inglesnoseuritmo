Fashion Vocabulary

Trend

A general direction in which something is developing or changing; what's "in" right now.

Vintage

Clothing from a previous era, typically at least 20 years old.

Sustainable

Eco-friendly fashion that focuses on ethical production and longevity.

Outfit

A set of clothes worn together, typically for a particular occasion.

Tailored

Clothing made or adjusted to fit a specific person perfectly.

Thrifting

The act of shopping at second-hand stores to find unique or cheaper items.

Attitude vs. Reality

Madonna says fashion and dancing are ways to escape the "pain of life". Do you agree that dressing up can change your mental state?

The phrase "Strike a pose" implies confidence. How much of fashion is about feeling confident versus just following a trend?

In the world of social media, do you think people are "vogueing" (posing) more than living their authentic lives?

Formal vs. Casual

Justin Timberlake sings about being "all pressed up". Do you enjoy dressing in formal clothes (suits, dresses) or do you find them uncomfortable?

The lyrics mention "fixed up to the nines" (meaning dressed very elegantly). On what occasions do you make an effort to look your best?

Thrifting & Sustainable Style

Macklemore says he looks "incredible" in second-hand clothes. Do you have any experience with thrifting or buying used items?

Why do you think vintage fashion has become so popular recently? Is it about the price or the uniqueness?

Do you think it's more ethical to buy second-hand clothes to avoid "fast fashion" waste?

Fashion Debate: Modern Issues

Fast Fashion vs. Sustainability

Should we stop buying from cheap brands like Shein to protect the environment, even if it's all some people can afford?

Dress Codes at Work

Are professional dress codes still necessary in 2024, or should people be allowed to wear whatever they want to the office?

Social Media Pressure

Does Instagram make us feel like we can't wear the same outfit twice? How does this affect our self-esteem?

Personal Brand

"You are what you wear." Do you agree that your style is a direct reflection of your personality?
