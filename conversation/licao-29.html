Scientific Terminology

Black Hole

A region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape.

Hypothesis

A proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for investigation.

Light-year

The distance that light travels in one year (about 9.46 trillion kilometers).

Quantum

The smallest discrete unit of a physical property, such as energy or matter.

Orbit

The curved path of a celestial object or spacecraft around a star, planet, or moon.

Empirical

Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.
