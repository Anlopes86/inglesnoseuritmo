Space Talk

To be light-years ahead

To be much more advanced than others.
“That telescope is light-years ahead of the old model.”

A giant leap

A very important advance or change.
“Reusable rockets were a giant leap for space travel.”

To explore the unknown

To investigate something unfamiliar or mysterious.

To launch a mission

To officially begin an organized project or expedition.

A long shot

An idea with a small chance of success.
“Finding life there is a long shot, but worth exploring.”
