Key Expressions

To binge-watch

To watch multiple episodes of a television program in rapid succession.
"I binge-watched the entire season of that new show in just one weekend."

A cliffhanger

A dramatic and exciting ending to an episode that leaves the audience in suspense.
"The season finale ended on such a cliffhanger; I can't wait for next year!"

To steal the show

To attract more attention and praise than other people in a performance.
"The supporting actor was so good that he completely stole the show from the lead."

A blockbuster

A movie that is very successful, especially one that costs a lot to make.
"The new superhero movie is expected to be the biggest blockbuster of the summer."

Behind the scenes

The things that happen away from public view, especially during the making of a movie.
"I love watching documentaries that show what happens behind the scenes on a movie set."

Spoiler alert

A warning that an important part of the plot of a movie or book is about to be revealed.
"Spoiler alert! If you haven't seen the movie yet, don't read this article."

The Streaming Revolution

Cinema vs. Streaming.

The way we consume stories has changed forever. We went from waiting a week for an episode to having everything at our fingertips.

The Traditionalist: Nothing beats the big screen, the smell of popcorn, and the shared experience of a dark theater.
The Modernist: I want to pause, rewind, and watch in my pajamas. Streaming gives more creators a chance to tell stories.

Is the 'magic of cinema' dying, or just evolving?
