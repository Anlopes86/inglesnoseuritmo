Key Expressions

To binge-watch

To watch multiple episodes of a series in one sitting.
"I stayed up all night to binge-watch the new season of Stranger Things."

A cliffhanger

An ending that leaves the audience in suspense.
"The first episode ended on such a cliffhanger; I have to see what happens next!"

To steal the show

To attract more attention and praise than other people in a performance.
"The supporting actor really stole the show with his incredible performance."

A blockbuster

A very successful and popular movie or book.
"The new superhero movie is expected to be a massive blockbuster."

Behind the scenes

The work or activities that happen away from public view.
"The documentary shows what really goes on behind the scenes of a movie set."

Spoiler alert

A warning that important plot details are about to be revealed.
"Spoiler alert! Don't read this if you haven't seen the finale yet."

The Streaming Revolution

Cinema vs. Streaming.

The way we consume pop culture has changed forever. We no longer wait a week for an episode; we have everything at our fingertips.

The Cinema View: Movies are meant to be a shared, large-scale experience in a dark theater.
The Streaming View: Comfort and choice are more important. Binge-watching at home is the new standard.

Is the 'magic of the movies' dying, or just evolving?
