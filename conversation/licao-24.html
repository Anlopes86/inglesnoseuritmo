Lesson 24: The Gig Economy & Remote Work

Warm-up: Working Hard

Let's talk about work:

If you didn't have to work for money, how would you spend your time?

Do you work to live, or do you live to work?

How many hours a day do you think is "healthy" for a person to work?

Does hard work always lead to success?

Work Vocabulary

To commute

To travel some distance between one's home and place of work regularly.

Burnout

State of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by excessive stress.

Side Hustle

A secondary job or business to earn extra income.

Overtime

Time worked in addition to one's normal working hours.

To quit

To leave a permanent job or a career.

Workaholic

A person who works compulsively and has trouble relaxing.
