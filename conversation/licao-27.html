Historical Vocabulary

Legacy

Something handed down from the past, as from an ancestor or predecessor.

Turning Point

A time when a decisive change in a situation occurs, especially one with beneficial results.

Heir

A person legally entitled to the property or rank of another on that person's death.

Ancient

Belonging to the very distant past and no longer in existence.

Milestone

An action or event marking a significant change or stage in development.

Outcome

The way a thing turns out; a consequence or result.
