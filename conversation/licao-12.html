Key Vocabulary & Expressions

To take a trip down memory lane

To remember and talk about things from the past.

The good old days

A nostalgic term for a happy time in the past.

A core memory

A very important and defining memory in your life.

To have a vague memory

To remember something unclearly.

To be etched in your memory

To be something you will never forget.

To look back on

To think about something in the past.
