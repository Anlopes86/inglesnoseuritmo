Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about work and career.

A nine-to-five job

A job with regular, traditional hours.

To climb the corporate ladder

To advance in one's career to higher positions.

A dead-end job

A job with no opportunities for promotion.

Work-life balance

The balance between time spent working and personal life.

To be your own boss

To be self-employed or have your own business.

To burn out

To become extremely tired or sick from working too hard.
