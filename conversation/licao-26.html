Key Psychological Terms

Resilience

The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; mental toughness.

Empathy

The ability to understand and share the feelings of another person.

Cognitive Bias

A systematic error in thinking that occurs when people are processing information.

Introvert / Extrovert

Terms describing where a person gets their energy from.

Defense Mechanism

An unconscious psychological strategy used to protect a person from anxiety.

Attachment Style

How we relate and connect to others (Secure, Anxious, or Avoidant).
