Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about the supernatural.

To send shivers down your spine

To make you feel very frightened or excited.

An unsolved mystery

A mystery that has not been solved.

To have a sixth sense

To have a special intuition or perception.

A gut feeling

An instinctive feeling, without a logical reason.

To debunk a myth

To prove that a popular belief is false.

An urban legend

A modern, scary story that is told as if it were true.
