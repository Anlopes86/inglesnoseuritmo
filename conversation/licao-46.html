Key Expressions

To fit in

To conform to the norms of a group.
"When I moved to a new school, I just wanted to fit in."

To stand out

To be easily noticeable or different from others.
"Her bright red hair makes her stand out in a crowd."

True to yourself

Acting in accordance with your own beliefs and values.
"It's important to stay true to yourself, no matter what others think."

To find yourself

To discover your true identity or purpose.
"He traveled to India to find himself."

Peer pressure

Influence from members of one's peer group.
"Many teenagers smoke because of peer pressure."

To break the mold

To do something differently from how it has always been done.
"She broke the mold by becoming the first female CEO of the company."

The Masks We Wear

Do we have one true identity, or many?

Most people adjust their behavior depending on their environment: work, family, friends, or online.

Authenticity: The goal is to be exactly the same person in every situation.
Adaptability: Wearing different 'masks' is a normal and necessary social skill.

Is adapting to fit in a form of lying, or just survival?
