Warm-up: Who Inspires You?

Let's talk about admiration, character, and influence.

Who is someone you admire deeply?

Do heroes need to be famous?

What qualities make someone a good role model?

Can a flawed person still be inspiring?

Key Expressions

Role model

A person whose behavior and values inspire others.

To stand up for

To defend or support a person, value, or cause.

Unsung hero

Someone who does good work without much recognition.

To lead by example

To influence others through your own actions.

To make a difference

To have a meaningful positive impact.

Larger than life

Very impressive or bigger than ordinary reality.

Everyday Heroes

Many heroes are invisible.

Parents, teachers, nurses, social workers, and friends often make a difference in quiet ways. They may never become famous, but they change lives deeply.

Public heroes: leaders, athletes, activists, artists.
Private heroes: family members, mentors, caregivers, friends.

Do we admire fame too much and overlook quiet courage?
