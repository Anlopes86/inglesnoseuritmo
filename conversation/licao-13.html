Lesson 13: Crime & Justice

Warm-up: About Justice

Let's start our conversation:

Why do you think people commit crimes?

If you could create one new law, what would it be?

Is our justice system fair?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about crime and justice.

To get away with murder

To not be caught or punished for a serious crime.

To do time

To serve a prison sentence.

To be caught red-handed

To be caught in the act of doing something wrong.

An inside job

A crime committed with help from someone inside the organization.

To turn a blind eye

To ignore something that you know is wrong.

The long arm of the law

The idea that the police will eventually catch criminals.
