Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about happiness.

On cloud nine

To be extremely happy and excited.

The simple things in life

The small, everyday pleasures.

To find your purpose

To discover your main goal or reason for living.

To live in the moment

To be fully present and not worry about the past or future.

To count your blessings

To be grateful for the good things in your life.

A means to an end

Something you do only to achieve something else (e.g., money).
