Key Expressions

To age gracefully

To grow older with confidence, dignity, and acceptance.

Senior moment

A humorous expression for forgetting something briefly.

Over the hill

An informal way to say someone is past their youth.

Young at heart

Having a lively and youthful attitude despite being older.

To slow down

To reduce your pace or level of activity.

To stand the test of time

To remain valuable or relevant over many years.

What Changes with Age?

Aging is physical, emotional, and social.

People often lose energy in some areas, but gain perspective, patience, resilience, and clarity in others.

Possible losses: speed, spontaneity, physical strength.
Possible gains: confidence, self-knowledge, emotional balance.

Is aging more about decline, or about transformation?
