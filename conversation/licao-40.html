Key Expressions

To fortify

To strengthen defenses. "We need to fortify the house."

Ration

To distribute limited supplies. "Let's ration food for a week."

Safe haven

A protected place. "We found a safe haven in the hills."

To scavenge

To search for usable items. "We scavenged an old supermarket."

Trust issues

Difficulty trusting others. "Trust issues are common after disasters."

Moral dilemma

A hard ethical choice. "Would you sacrifice one to save many?"

Discussion

Who would survive longer: teachers or doctors?

Would you help strangers?

Where would you hide?

Activity: Choose Your Team

Pick 5 people for your survivor group from the list and justify your choices: athlete, scientist, chef, mechanic, influencer, soldier, teacher.

Discuss roles, strengths and weaknesses and create a short plan for the first 72 hours.
