Key Expressions

To make a living

To earn enough money to support oneself.
"He makes a living as a freelance photographer."

A dead-end job

A job with no prospects for promotion or advancement.
"She quit her dead-end job to start her own business."

To climb the corporate ladder

To advance in one's career within a company.
"He's working hard to climb the corporate ladder."

To be your own boss

To be self-employed or run your own business.
"The best part of freelancing is being your own boss."

To work nine to five

To work regular office hours.
"I'm tired of working nine to five; I need more flexibility."

To think outside the box

To think creatively or unconventionally.
"In this unusual career, you really need to think outside the box."
