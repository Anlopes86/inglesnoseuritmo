Lesson 16: The Future & AI

Warm-up: The Future is Now

Let's start our conversation:

Are you excited or worried about the future of Artificial Intelligence?

If you could ask a super-intelligent AI one question, what would it be?

Will AI create more jobs than it destroys?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about the future and AI.

Cutting-edge

The most modern and advanced stage of something.

Game-changer

An idea or event that significantly changes a situation.

To think outside the box

To think in an original and creative way.

A paradigm shift

A fundamental change in approach or underlying assumptions.

The sky is the limit

There is no limit to what can be achieved.

To open a can of worms

To create a situation that will cause a lot of problems.
