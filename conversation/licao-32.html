Key Expressions

To ghost someone

To suddenly stop all communication without explanation.

On the same page

To share the same expectations or understanding.

A rough patch

A difficult moment in a relationship.

To hit it off

To connect immediately and naturally with someone.

To see eye to eye

To agree fully about something important.

Soulmate

A person who feels like your ideal emotional match.

Love in the 21st Century

Technology creates access, but it also creates confusion.

People can connect faster than ever, but they also face ghosting, mixed signals, and the pressure of constant communication. Modern love often depends on emotional clarity more than availability.

Healthy digital habits: honest intentions, boundaries, consistency, and respect.
Unhealthy digital habits: disappearing, breadcrumbing, emotional ambiguity, and control.

Has love become easier to find, or just easier to fake?
