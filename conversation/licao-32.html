Modern Love Terminology

To ghost someone

To suddenly stop all communication with someone without explanation.

On the same page

To have the same understanding or intentions in a relationship.

A rough patch

A difficult period or a crisis in a relationship.

To hit it off

To have an immediate connection or liking for someone upon meeting.

To see eye to eye

To agree fully with someone on a specific topic or value.

Soulmate

A person ideally suited to another as a close friend or romantic partner.
