Useful Expressions

Flip the cards to learn the 6 expressions of the day. Each card includes an example sentence.

To shoot for the stars

To aim for a very high or ambitious goal.

"She always tells her students to shoot for the stars and never give up on their dreams."

A lifelong dream

A dream you've had for your entire life.

"Becoming a doctor has been a lifelong dream of mine since childhood."

To come true

When a dream becomes a reality.

"After years of hard work, my dream of opening my own business finally came true."

To set a goal

To decide on something you want to achieve.

"I set a goal to learn English fluently within two years."

To chase a dream

To pursue something you really want.

"He decided to chase his dream of becoming a musician, despite his family's concerns."

Beyond my wildest dreams

Better than anything I could imagine.

"Winning the lottery was beyond my wildest dreams; I still can't believe it happened!"
