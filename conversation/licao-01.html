Conversation: Dreams & Ambitions

Warm-up Questions

Let's start our conversation. Think about these questions and share your thoughts.

  • What did you want to be when you were a child?
  • What’s your biggest dream today?
  • Should people follow their dreams no matter what?

Useful Expressions

Click on the cards to flip them and see the meaning.

To shoot for the stars

To aim for a very high or ambitious goal.

"She's always shooting for the stars."

A lifelong dream

A dream you've had for your entire life.

"Visiting Japan is a lifelong dream of mine."

To come true

When a dream becomes a reality.

"His dream finally came true."

To set a goal

To decide on something you want to achieve.

"I've set a goal to run a marathon."
