Ambition vs. Reality

Big dreams need structure.

Having ambition is inspiring, but without a plan it can stay only a fantasy. That's why people need to set goals, measure progress, and stay flexible when life changes.

Healthy ambition: motivates you, gives direction, helps you grow.
Unhealthy ambition: creates anxiety, comparison, burnout, and guilt.

Is it better to dream bigger than your reality, or stay realistic from the beginning?
