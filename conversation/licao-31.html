Does "culture" only mean high art, or is a football match culture too?

Key Leisure Terms

Flavor of the month

Something currently popular, often briefly.

Mainstream

Ideas, attitudes, or activities that are shared by most people; "popular culture".

It's not my cup of tea

Something one does not like or enjoy.

Binge-watch

To watch multiple episodes of a TV program in rapid succession.

A melting pot

A place where different cultures mix and interact.

Niche

Interests that appeal to a small, specialized section of the population.

Song 1 Debate

"Adventure of a Lifetime" - Feeling Alive & Leisure

Personal

The song says "everything you want's a dream away." Do you agree that leisure activities can make you feel truly alive?

Culture

Is your leisure time more active (sports, travel, adventure) or more passive (movies, reading, gaming)? Which do you think is healthier?

Opinion

Do you think modern life makes it harder for people to truly unwind? Are we too distracted by our phones?

Debate

What is the most breathtaking cultural event you have ever attended? Did it change the way you see leisure?

The Streaming Revolution

Leisure then vs. now:

We no longer wait a week for a new episode. We binge-watch entire seasons in a weekend.

Pros: Choice, convenience, and original content (Netflix, HBO).
Cons: Social isolation, "choice fatigue," and the end of shared cultural moments.

Is the cinema dying, or will it always be a "masterpiece" experience?

Song 2 Debate

"Sunny Afternoon" - Leisure, Money & Simple Pleasures

Personal

The singer lost everything but still enjoys his sunny afternoon. What free leisure activities make you happiest? Do you need money to truly unwind?

Society

Modern leisure is often expensive (streaming subscriptions, travel, dining out). Is mainstream culture becoming a luxury only the rich can afford?

Lifestyle

The song is from the 1960s. Do you think people in the past were better at enjoying simple pleasures than we are today?

Hot Take

"Lazing on a sunny afternoon" — is doing nothing a valid form of leisure, or is it just laziness? Where's the line?

High Culture vs. Pop Culture

Some people say Opera is "better" than Pop music. Do you think there is a hierarchy in culture?

Why do some mainstream movies become global hits while others fail?

If you had a free Sunday, would you rather go sightseeing at a historic monument or stay home and watch a documentary?

Song 3 Debate

"Time of Our Lives" - Living in the Moment & Having Fun

Personal

The song says "this is the last twenty dollars I got, but I'mma have a good time." Have you ever spent your last money on a leisure activity? Was it worth it?

Culture

Nightlife, parties, and clubs are a huge part of mainstream leisure culture. Is going out the best way to unwind, or is it overrated?

Opinion

Pitbull says "forget about your problems." Is leisure an escape from reality or a healthy way to recharge? Can ignoring problems actually help?

Debate

Should you save money or spend it on experiences? Is it irresponsible to prioritize fun over financial security, or is life too short to only worry about bills?

The Power of Hobbies

Fact: Hobbies can reduce stress and improve mental health.

1. Do you have a niche hobby that most people don't know about?

2. Have you ever tried to turn a hobby into a side hustle (business)?

3. Is there a skill you've always wanted to learn (like playing an instrument)?

"A hobby a day keeps the burnout away."
