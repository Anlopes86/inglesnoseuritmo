Media & Political Terms

Bias

Prejudice in favor of or against one thing, person, or group compared with another.

Echo Chamber

An environment where a person only encounters information that reflects their own opinions.

Clickbait

Sensationalist headlines designed to tempt users to click on a link.

To Fact-check

To investigate an issue in order to verify the facts.

Partisan

A strong supporter of a party, cause, or person, often showing unreasoning bias.

Watchdog

A person or group (like the media) that keeps a close watch on the government to prevent corruption.
