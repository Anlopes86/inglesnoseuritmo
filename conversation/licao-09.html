Lesson 9: Technology & Social Media

Warm-up: Our Digital Lives

Let's start our conversation:

Is technology making our lives better or worse?

How much time do you spend on social media?

Could you live without your phone for a week?

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

Click the cards to explore ideas about technology.

To be glued to a screen

To spend too much time looking at a screen.

A double-edged sword

Something with both advantages and disadvantages.

Information overload

Having too much information to process.

To scroll endlessly

Scrolling social media without a specific goal.

FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)

Anxiety that an exciting event is happening elsewhere.

Digital detox

A period of time without using electronic devices.
