Useful Learning Expressions

Flip each card, then give a quick personal example.

To be a quick study

To learn or understand something quickly.

To pick something up

To learn a skill naturally, often through experience.

To brush up on

To improve a skill or knowledge you learned before.

Trial and error

Learning by trying different solutions and correcting mistakes.

To connect the dots

To understand how separate facts or ideas relate.

Hands-on learning

Learning by actively doing and experimenting.
