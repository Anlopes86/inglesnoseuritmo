Key Expressions

To grow up

To develop from being a child into an adult.

To look up to someone

To admire and respect someone as a role model.

Child at heart

An adult who still has a playful, youthful spirit.

Coming-of-age

The process of growing into an adult and gaining maturity.

To fit in

To feel accepted and belong to a group.

To go through a phase

To experience a temporary period of behavior or interest.

Childhood Then and Now

Growing up has changed.

Many adults say childhood used to be more free, less digital, and more connected to outdoor play. At the same time, children today often have more information, more opportunities, and more pressure.

Then: more street games, less screen time, slower communication.
Now: more technology, earlier maturity, stronger online influence.

Are kids losing their childhood, or is childhood simply changing?
