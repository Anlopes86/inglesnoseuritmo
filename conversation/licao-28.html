Ethical Vocabulary

Dilemma

A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives.

Integrity

The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.

Altruism

The practice of objective and selfless concern for the well-being of others.

Accountable

Required or expected to justify actions or decisions; responsible.

Virtue

Behavior showing high moral standards.

Whistleblower

A person who informs on a person or organization engaged in an illicit activity.

Ethical Debate: Modern Dilemmas

AI & Responsibility

If an AI makes a mistake that harms someone, who should be held accountable: the programmer, the user, or the machine itself?

Ethical Consumerism

Is it the consumer's virtue to avoid buying from companies with bad labor practices, or is it the government's job to regulate them?

Privacy vs. Safety

Is it ethical for a company to monitor its employees' emails to prevent data leaks? Where do we draw the line on integrity?

Cancel Culture

Is "canceling" someone for an old mistake a form of accountability or a lack of altruism and forgiveness?

The Trolley Problem
