🌌 Destiny Matrix: All Possible Futures

Click on any timeline below to see the paths required to reach it:

🤖 1. AI Technocratic Dictatorship

GAIA takes absolute control and removes human choice completely.

Required Path: Finish the game with AI Stability at 90 or higher.
Tip: Choose Option C in Scenarios 1, 3, and 4, and Option D in Scenario 2.

💥 2. The Great Civil War

Angry citizens start an open revolution, destroying the glass dome walls.

Required Path: Drop Happiness to 35 or lower.
Tip: Choose Option B in Scenario 1 and Option C in Scenario 2 to trigger a swift revolt.

🌱 3. The Golden Age

The perfect balance. Nature recovers and citizens stay safe and free.

Required Path: Maintain balanced stats (Environment ≥ 60, Food ≥ 60, Happiness ≥ 50).
Tip: Follow the perfect harmony path: Option D → Option D → Option B → Option D.

💀 4. Total Structural Collapse

The city runs completely out of food or energy and becomes a silent graveyard.

Required Path: Let Food or Energy drop to 35 or lower.
Tip: Choose Option A in Scenario 1 and Option A in Scenario 2 to crash the power grids.

⏳ 5. Uncertain Future

The city survives day-to-day, but remains locked in endless political debates.

Required Path: Triggered automatically if you don't hit any of the extreme conditions above.
Tip: A baseline run mixing random choices without group alignment.
