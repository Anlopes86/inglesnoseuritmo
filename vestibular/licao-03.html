Warm Up

Apresentando a Família

Clique nos cards para ver a tradução.

Ela é minha mãe.

He is my father.

Ele é meu pai.

He is my brother.

Dialogue

Ana: Kenji, this is my friend, Sofia.

Kenji: Hello, Sofia. Nice to meet you.

Sofia: Hi, Kenji. Nice to meet you too.

Ana: We are friends from school.

Grammar: Possessive Pronouns (My/Your)

Complete as frases:

1. This is friend, Sofia.

2. What is name?

3. name is Kenji.
