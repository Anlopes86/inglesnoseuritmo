Lesson 01: The Foundation: Verb To Be

Warm-up: Who Are They?

Olhe as imagens. Usando "He", "She" e "They", o que você pode dizer sobre estas pessoas?

Name: John
From: USA
Feeling: Happy

Name: Maria

Exemplo: "He is John. He is from the USA. He is happy."

Presentation: To Be (Affirmative)

O verbo "To Be" é usado para descrever quem somos, o que somos, como estamos e onde estamos.

Forma Completa

  • I am a teacher.
  • You are a student.
  • He is happy.
  • She is from Brazil.
  • It is cold today.
  • We are friends.
  • They are at home.

Contrações (Contractions)

  • I'm a teacher.
  • You're a student.
  • He's happy.
  • She's from Brazil.
  • It's cold today.
  • We're friends.
  • They're at home.

Grammar Focus: Negative & Interrogative

Forma Negativa

Adicione "not" depois do verbo.

  • He is not a doctor. (He isn't...)
  • They are not sad. (They aren't...)
  • I am not tired. (I'm not...)

Forma Interrogativa

Inverta o verbo com o sujeito.

  • Is he a doctor? (Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.)
  • Are they sad? (Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.)
  • Am I late? (Yes, you are. / No, you aren't.)
