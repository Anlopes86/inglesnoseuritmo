Lesson 03: Right Now: Present Continuous

Warm-up: What Are They Doing?

Olhe a imagem. O que as pessoas estão fazendo neste exato momento?

Exemplo: "A woman is doing yoga. A man is running."

Presentation: Present Continuous

Usamos o Present Continuous para ações acontecendo AGORA ou em um período temporário.

Estrutura: Sujeito + Verbo To Be (am/is/are) + Verbo-ING

  • I am working now.
  • You are studying English.
  • He is watching TV.
  • She is listening to music.
  • It is raining outside.
  • We are having dinner.
  • They are playing soccer.

Grammar Focus: Spelling with "-ING"

A terminação "-ing" tem algumas regras de ortografia:

  • Regra Geral: Adicione -ing
    go going  |  talk talking  |  read reading
  • Verbos terminados em -e: Remova o -e, adicione -ing
    make making  |  have having  |  dance dancing
  • CVC (Consoante-Vogal-Consoante): Dobre a última consoante
    run running  |  sit sitting  |  swim swimming

Grammar Focus: Negative & Interrogative

A estrutura é a mesma do verbo "to be" que já aprendemos!

Negative (am/is/are + not)

You are not (aren't) listening.

He is not (isn't) sleeping.

I am not playing.

Interrogative (am/is/are + Sujeito)

Are you listening?

Is he sleeping?

Are they coming?

Practice 3: Routine vs. Now

Escolha o tempo verbal correto: Simple Present ou Present Continuous.

1. I usually (drink) tea, but today I (drink) coffee.

2. She (work) in a hospital, but she (not / work) this week.

3. They normally (play) tennis on Sundays, but it's raining now, so they (watch) a movie.

Speaking: Describe the Scene

Descreva o que está acontecendo na sala de aula agora. O que o professor está fazendo? E você?

  • What is the teacher doing? (He/She is...)
  • What are you doing? (I am...)
  • Are you writing?
  • Is the teacher speaking English or Portuguese?
  • Are we learning a new topic?
