Warm-up: Can you count it?

Coffee
Apples
Money
Suggestions
Time
Books
Water
Work

Dica de Intermediário: "Money" é incontável, mas "Dollar" é contável!

Much vs. Many vs. A Lot Of

MANY

Para Contáveis em perguntas e negativas.

"How many friends?"

MUCH

Para Incontáveis em perguntas e negativas.

"How much time?"

A LOT OF

Usado para AMBOS, principalmente em frases afirmativas.

"I have a lot of work."

Small Amounts: A few & A little

Usamos estas palavras para falar de quantidades pequenas.

A few (+ Countable)

  • "I have a few questions."
  • "She has a few friends here."

A little (+ Uncountable)

  • "I have a little sugar."
  • "Wait a little time."

Practice: Choose Wisely

1. How people are coming?

2. We don't have information yet.

3. I only need milk for my coffee.

4. There are cookies left.

5. He has experience in this area.

Speaking Challenge

Responda honestamente para praticar:

  • How much water do you drink a day?
  • How many apps do you have on your phone?
  • Do you have a lot of free time on weekends?
  • Are there a few places you still want to visit?
