Presentation: Simple Past (Regular Verbs)

Usamos o Simple Past para ações que começaram e terminaram no passado.

Palavras-chave: yesterday, last night/week/month/year, two days ago.

Estrutura: Sujeito + Verbo-ED

A melhor parte: a forma do verbo é a mesma para TODOS os sujeitos!

  • I worked a lot yesterday.
  • You played very well.
  • He visited his parents last week.
  • She loved the movie.
  • We danced all night.
  • They traveled to Japan two years ago.

Grammar Focus: Spelling with "-ED"

A terminação "-ed" também tem regras de ortografia:

  • Regra Geral: Adicione -ed
    work worked  |  play played  |  listen listened
  • Verbos terminados em -e: Adicione -d
    live lived  |  love loved  |  dance danced
  • Consoante + y: Troque "y" por -ied
    study studied  |  try tried  |  cry cried
  • CVC (Consoante-Vogal-Consoante): Dobre a última consoante
    stop stopped  |  plan planned

Presentation: Irregular Verbs

Muitos verbos comuns são irregulares e não seguem a regra do "-ed". Eles precisam ser memorizados!

Presente (Base Form) Passado (Simple Past)
go went
have had
do did
say said
see saw
eat ate

Grammar Focus: Negative & Interrogative

Para negativas e perguntas, usamos o auxiliar DID. O verbo principal volta à forma base (sem "-ed" e sem ser irregular)!

Negative (didn't + verbo base)

I didn't work yesterday.

She didn't go to the party.

They didn't see the movie.

Interrogative (Did + sujeito + verbo base)

Did you work yesterday?

Did she go to the party?

Did they see the movie?

Practice 1: Affirmative Sentences

Complete as frases com o passado simples dos verbos.

1. I (visit) my grandparents last weekend.

2. She (study) a lot for the test.

3. They (go) to the cinema yesterday.

4. He (eat) a big sandwich for lunch.

5. We (stop) at the red light.

6. You (have) a meeting this morning.

Practice 2: Questions & Negatives

Transforme as frases em negativas e interrogativas.

She watched a movie.

(Negative) She a movie.

(Question) a movie?

They went to the party.

(Negative) They to the party.

(Question) to the party?

Speaking: What Did You Do?

Responda às perguntas do professor sobre seu passado recente.

  • What did you do yesterday?
  • Did you watch TV last night? What did you watch?
  • What did you eat for breakfast this morning?
  • Did you travel last year? Where did you go?
  • Did you see your friends last weekend?

Homework: My Last Weekend

Escreva um parágrafo (5-7 frases) sobre o que você fez no último fim de semana. Use pelo menos 3 verbos regulares e 3 verbos irregulares.

Exemplo:

"Last weekend, I visited my aunt. I helped her in the garden. In the afternoon, we watched a movie. For dinner, she made a delicious pasta. We ate together and had a great time."
