Lesson 09: Describing Places

Warm-up: What's in the Room?

Olhe a imagem e liste 5 objetos que você consegue ver.

Exemplo: "I see a sofa, a lamp, a window..."

Presentation: "There is" & "There are"

Para dizer que algo existe em um lugar, usamos There is e There are.

There is (There's) - Singular

Use para UMA coisa.

  • There is a sofa in the living room.
  • There's a cat on the chair.
  • Is there a window? Yes, there is.
  • There isn't a TV.

There are - Plural

Use para DUAS ou MAIS coisas.

  • There are two chairs.
  • There are some books on the table.
  • Are there any pictures? Yes, there are.
  • There aren't any people.

Presentation: Prepositions of Place

Preposições nos dizem ONDE as coisas estão.

IN
ON
UNDER
NEXT TO
BETWEEN
BEHIND

Practice 1: Complete the Sentences

Complete com "is", "are", "isn't", ou "aren't".

1. There a big window in my bedroom.

2. There three people in the car.

3. A: Is there a cat? B: No, there .

4. There any good movies on TV tonight.

5. A: Are there any apples? B: Yes, there .

Practice 2: Where is it?

Olhe a imagem e complete as frases com a preposição correta.

1. The cat is the sofa.

2. The table is the sofa.

3. The ball is the table.

Speaking: Describe Your Room

Agora é sua vez! Descreva o cômodo onde você está agora.

  • What is there on your desk? (There is...)
  • How many windows are there in this room? (There are...)
  • Is there a TV in front of you?
  • What is next to your computer?

Homework: My Dream Room

Descreva seu quarto dos sonhos. O que existe nele? Onde estão os objetos? Escreva 5-7 frases usando "There is/are" e pelo menos 4 preposições diferentes.

Exemplo:

"In my dream room, there is a big window. There are a lot of plants next to the window. There is a comfortable sofa in front of the TV. A small cat is sleeping under the sofa..."

You're a Decorator!

You can now describe any place with confidence. Great job!
