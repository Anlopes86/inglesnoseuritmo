Lesson 14: Phrasal Verbs

Warm-up: Small Words, Big Change

A phrasal verb is a verb plus a small word like up, on, out or for.

That small word changes the meaning:

  • pick = choose or lift
  • pick up = collect someone / learn quickly / lift

In Essentials, the goal is recognition and practical use, not memorizing giant lists.
