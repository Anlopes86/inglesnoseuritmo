Lesson 05: Past Continuous

Warm-up: What Happened?

Olhe a imagem. O que o homem estava fazendo antes de cair?

Pense em uma frase como: "He was painting the wall."

Presentation: Past Continuous

Usamos o Past Continuous para uma ação que estava em progresso em um momento específico no passado.

Estrutura: Sujeito + was/were + Verbo-ING

  • At 8 PM last night, I was watching a movie.
  • He was working when I called.
  • They were playing tennis all morning yesterday.

Grammar Focus: "When" & "While"

Usamos "When" e "While" para conectar ações no passado.

WHEN Short Action

Usamos "when" para uma ação curta (Simple Past) que interrompe uma ação longa (Past Continuous).

Exemplo:

I was taking a shower when the phone rang.

WHILE Long Action

Usamos "while" para duas ações longas (Past Continuous) acontecendo ao mesmo tempo.

Exemplo:

I was cooking while my brother was watching TV.

Practice 1: Complete the Story

Use Simple Past ou Past Continuous para completar as frases.

1. I (study) when my friend (call).

2. While she (sleep), the dog (play) in the garden.

3. He (break) his leg when he (ski).

4. They (drive) home when they (see) the accident.

Speaking: Detective Game

Vamos jogar! Um "crime" aconteceu ontem às 21h. O professor é o detetive e você é o suspeito.

  • Detective: What were you doing at 9 PM last night?
  • Suspect: I was...
  • Detective: Were you watching TV?
  • Suspect: Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.
  • Detective: What was your family doing?

Homework: An Interrupted Story

Escreva um parágrafo (5-7 frases) descrevendo algo interessante ou engraçado que aconteceu. Comece sua história com "I was..." e use "when" e "while".

Exemplo:

"Last night, I was cooking dinner while my son was doing his homework. Suddenly, I heard a strange noise. I was walking to the living room when I saw that my cat opened the window and was playing outside!"
