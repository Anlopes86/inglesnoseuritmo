Lesson 15: Conjunctions

Warm-up: Join the Ideas

Conjunctions connect ideas and make English sound more complete.

and = add information
but = contrast
because = give a reason
so = show a result

Rule Review

and: I study at night and I review in the morning.

but: She is tired, but she still wants to finish the task.

because: We stayed home because it was raining.

so: It was raining, so we stayed home.

when: Call me when you arrive.

if: We will start early if everyone is ready.

Practice 1: Choose the Connector

1. I was late, ___ the bus arrived very slowly.

2. We can go out now, ___ it stopped raining.

3. He studied a lot, ___ he felt ready for the test.

Practice 2: Rewrite the Pair

Join the sentences with the best conjunction.

1. I was hungry. I made a sandwich.

2. She likes music. She doesn't like loud concerts.

Production

Write a short paragraph about your routine or plans. Use at least 4 conjunctions from the lesson.
