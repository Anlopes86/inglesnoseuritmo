Presentation: Simple Present

Usamos o Simple Present para falar sobre rotinas, hábitos e fatos universais.

I / You / We / They + VERB

Rotina: I drink coffee every morning.

Hábito: We play soccer on Saturdays.

Fato: You live in Brazil.

Fato: They speak Portuguese.

Exemplo extra: My friends go to the cinema often.

He / She / It + VERB-S

Rotina: She wakes up at 7 AM.

Hábito: He watches a lot of series.

Fato: The Earth goes around the Sun.

Fato: It rains a lot in the Amazon.

Exemplo extra: My brother likes to cook.

Grammar Focus: The "-s" Rule

Para He, She & It, a terminação do verbo muda:

  • Regra Geral: Adicione -s
    work works  |  live lives  |  see sees  |  eat eats
  • Verbos terminados em -o, -ss, -sh, -ch, -x: Adicione -es
    go goes  |  watch watches  |  kiss kisses  |  teach teaches
  • Consoante + y: Troque "y" por -ies
    study studies  |  try tries  |  cry cries  |  fly flies
  • Exceção Importante:
    have has

Grammar Focus: Negative & Interrogative

Para negativas e perguntas, usamos os auxiliares DO e DOES. O verbo principal volta à forma base!

Negative

I / You / We / They + don't + VERBO

You don't work here.

We don't like spicy food.

He / She / It + doesn't + VERBO

She doesn't work here.

It doesn't rain much in the desert.

Interrogative

Do + I / You / We / They + VERBO?

Do you work here?

Do they play video games?

Does + He / She / It + VERBO?

Does she work here?

Does he like coffee?

Practice 1: Conjugate the Verbs

Coloque os verbos entre parênteses na forma afirmativa correta.

1. He (work) in a bank.

2. They (live) in a big house.

3. My sister (study) French.

4. I (watch) movies on Fridays.

5. The class (finish) at 9 PM.

6. You (have) a beautiful car.

7. My cat (sleep) a lot.

Practice 2: Questions & Negatives

Transforme as frases afirmativas em negativas ou perguntas.

He works here.

(Negative) He here.

(Question) here?

They like vegetables.

(Negative) They vegetables.

(Question) vegetables?

She speaks English well.

(Negative) She English well.

(Question) English well?

We go to the beach in summer.

(Negative) We to the beach in summer.

(Question) to the beach in summer?

Practice 3: Find the Mistake

Encontre o erro em cada frase e reescreva-a corretamente.

4. My brother studyes medicine.

Homework: A Special Routine

Escreva um parágrafo (4-6 frases) descrevendo a rotina diária de seu/sua celebridade, personagem ou parente favorito(a). Lembre-se da regra do "-s"!

Exemplo:

"My mother wakes up early every day. She drinks coffee and reads the news. She works at a hospital. She loves her job. In the evening, she watches TV with my father."
