Comparatives: Rule Review

Adjetivos Curtos (1 sílaba)

Adicione -er + than

  • Old → Older than
  • Big → Bigger than
  • Fast → Faster than

Adjetivos Longos (2+ sílabas)

Use more + adj + than

  • Famous → More famous than
  • Expensive → More expensive than
  • Modern → More modern than

Irregular: Good → Better | Bad → Worse | Far → Farther

Superlatives: The Best!

Usamos para comparar um item com todo um grupo.

The + Adj + -est

"Mount Everest is the highest mountain."

The most + Adj

"This is the most interesting book I've read."

Irregular: The Best | The Worst | The Farthest

Practice: Comparatives

1. Italy is (hot) than Norway.

2. A Ferrari is (expensive) than a Fiat.

3. This movie is (good) than the last one.

4. English is (easy) than Chinese.

Practice: Superlatives

1. He is the (tall) boy in class.

2. This is the (beautiful) place ever.

3. It was the (bad) day of my life.

4. Which is the (big) city in the world?
